Idigo naa sevakudu aatmapurnudu
Aatmapurnudu naa praanapriyudu (2)
Nenerparachukonina neetimantudu
Ne naadukonuvaadu naa daasudu (2)

Verse 1:
1. Aruvadu viruvadu vattinaarpadu
Dharmaanni sthaapinchunu
Swarame tana karavaalam vibhajinchunugaa
Merugaina ambugaa cheyabadenugaa
Tarataramula prajalakai nibandhanaayenu
Arudaina sevato alarinchunugaa
||idigo||

Verse 2:
2. Andhulanu badhirulanu baagucheyunu
Bandhakamulu vidipinchunu
Andhakaaramanta baapi velugu nichunu
Mandagiladu ennadu nalagudupadadu
Andarikai velugugaa velasi vachenu
Bhudigantamulaku bodha vyaapinchunu
||idigo||
