Kadalirammu kraistava yuvakaa kalasi rammu kreesturaaju sevalo
Kudiyedamulu chudaka kreestuvaipu chuchuchu
Teevramugaa takshaname taraliraa

Verse 1:
1. Lokanaadhudentayo lokulan jayinchuchunde
Lokulantavaani krinda longi krungi kumuluchunde
||teevra||

Verse 2:
2. Yesu naadhudentayo vedanato pilachuchunde
Erugamanchu lokulantaa verudaari povuchunde
||teevra||

Verse 3:
3. Mee yavvana jeevitaale kreestu sangha stambhaalu
Mee unnata saakshyaale mana prajalaku maargaalu
||teevra||

Verse 4:
4. Sodhanale vachinaa sramalenno chuttinaa
Saktini prabhuvichunu sadayudu sandehamela
||teevra||

Verse 5:
5. Satyavaakya khadgamuche satruvu nodinchutaku
Yesuvaarta chaatuchu sanghamu poshinchutaku
||teevra||
