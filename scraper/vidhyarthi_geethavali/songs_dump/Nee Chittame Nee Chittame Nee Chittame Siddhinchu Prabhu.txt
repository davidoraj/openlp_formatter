Nee chittame nee chittame
Nee chittame siddhinchu prabhu
Ee jeevitam nee kosame
Nee aatmato nimpu prabhu

Verse 1:
1. Krayadhanamu gaanu nee praanamichi
Nannu vidipinchitive
Nannu preminchi naa bhaaramu mosi
Nanu vimochinchitive
||nee chittame||

Verse 2:
2. Nee daasudanu aalakintunu
Nee swaramu vinipinchumu
Naato maatlaadi nannu balaparachi
Nee trovanu nadipinchumu
||nee chittame||

Verse 3:
3. Parichaaramu cheyan
Paricharyanu cheyan
Pani nappaginchitive
Paapamu nedirinchi
Phalitamu saadhinchi
Nee ishtamu neraverchedan
||nee chittame||
