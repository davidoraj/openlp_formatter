Schale em
Devuni koraku naa praanamu trushnagonuchunnadi
Duppi neeti vaagulakai aasapadunatlugaa
Aadarana, aanandamu aayanalo anudinamu

Verse 1:
1. Devuni sannidhiki neneppudu vattunani
Niratamu aayana premakorakai daahamu gonuchunnadi
||aadarana||

Verse 2:
2. Maranapu paasamulu nanu chuttukoni yundagaa
Vidipinchi jeevamuloniki nannu nadipinchenu yesu
||aadarana||

Verse 3:
3. Divaaraatramulu daiva vaakyadhyaanamu korakai
Praardhana sahavaasambu korakai aakali gonuchunnadi
||aadarana||

Verse 4:
4. Bhakti heenudanaiyunda balaheenudanai yundagaa
Aatmaabhishekamu nichi kreestu saakshigaa jese nannu
||aadarana||

Verse 5:
5. Paapulan parikimpagaa naalo prasava vedana kalige
Aatmalan sampaadinchavalenanu daahamu gonuchunnadi
||aadarana||

Verse 6:
6. Halleluya stotramul prabhu yesunake mahima
Niratamu stutulu keerti ghanata aayana naamamuke
||aadarana||
