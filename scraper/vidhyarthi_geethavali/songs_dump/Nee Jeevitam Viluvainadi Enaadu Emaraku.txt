Je. devaraaju
Schale bm
Nee jeevitam viluvainadi enaadu emaraku
Sreeyesunaamam neekento kshemam eenaade yochinchumaa
O nestamaa teliyunaa prabhu yesu ninnu preminchenu
Naa nestamaa telisiko prabhu yesu neekai maraninchenu
||nee jeevitam||

Verse 1:
1. Balamaina penugaali veechi alalento paipaiki lechi
Viluvaina nee jeevita naava talakrindulai vaalipova
Valadu bhayamu neekela kaladu yese neetodu
Yesu maraninchi marilechenu ninnu preminchi darijerchunu
||nee jeevitam||

Verse 2:
2. Gaadhaandhakaarampu loyalo vadagaali vadi savvadilo
Nadayaade neejeevita trova sudivadi nee adugulu tadabadegaa
Valadu bhayamu neekela kaladu yese neetodu
Yesu maraninchi marilechenu ninnu preminchi darijerchunu
||nee jeevitam||

Verse 3:
3. Kanaleni gamyambu kori enaleni kashtaalapaalai
Manaleni neejeevita gaadha kalalanni kanneeti vyadhale
Valadu bhayamu neekela kaladu yese neetodu
Yesu maraninchi marilechenu ninnu preminchi darijerchunu
||nee jeevitam||
