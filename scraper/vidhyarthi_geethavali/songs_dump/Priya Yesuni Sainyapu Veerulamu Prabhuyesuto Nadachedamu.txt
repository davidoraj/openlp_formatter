Schale g
Priya yesuni sainyapu veerulamu prabhuyesuto nadachedamu
Tana rudhirapu adugula jaadalalo vijayambuto saagedamu
Tana divya yavvana raktamunu chindinchenu maa koraku
Maa yavvana jeevita mesunikai arpintumu mudamuganu

Verse 1:
1. Paapapu chelagaatamulu sunkara sahavaasamulu
Saataanunito gala snehamulu valadani prabhu cheritimi
Prabhu yesu vasiyinchaganu maa dehame gehamugaa
Prabhu yesukai jeevinchedamu lokamu tyajiyinchedamu
||priya||

Verse 2:
2. Nasiyinchina aatmalakai maa yedalo bhaaramuto
Payaninchedamu sramiyinchedamu prabhuyesu sahaayamuto
Balaparchu yesuni battiyegaa bheekaramagu kaaryamula
Prabhuyesukai saadhinchedamu aatmala naarjinchedamu
||priya||

Verse 3:
3. Maa vijaya ninaadamulu sangeeta sunaadamulu
Yuvajanamulalo vinipinchedamu prati gruha praanganamulalo
Pravanudu prabhu kreestuni chetulalo balamaina yodhulugaa
Saataanu kotalu kulchedamu prabhudhvajamu naatedamu
||priya||
