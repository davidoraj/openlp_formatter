Verse 1:

Sampurnulagutaku balamichu devaa

Verse 2:
2. Anaadhalandari naadarinchuvaada
Annaartula nila poshinchuvaada
Bandhitulanu yila vidipinchuvaada
Baadhitulaku nee nyaayamichuvaada
||halleluya||

Verse 3:
3. Dayaakireetam dharanichitivi
Doshapu phalamunu tolaginchitivi
Nutana yavvana balamosagitivi
Nutana santasa melosagitivi
||halleluya||
