Parinati leni nee hrudayam
Phalamulu leni jeevitam
Parikinchumu arpinchumu
Sajeeva yaagamuga

Verse 1:
1. Jeevamu leni kriyalanu vidachi
Sampurnamagutaku prabhulo nilachi
Saagipo saakshigaa
Samarame jeevitam
Dayaa prema kshamaa sahanam (2)
Aatma parinatiye
||parinati||

Verse 2:
2. Paripakvamaina paapam maranam
Parisuddhudaina yese saranam
Parimalam paravasam
Yesuto jeevitam
Natana maani manasu maari (2)
Rupaantaram nondumu
||parinati||
