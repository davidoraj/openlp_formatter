Je. devaraaju
Schale e
Maa yesu maa aasa
Maa raajaa maa tejaa
Maa devaa mahaadeva
Jeevaadhaara maa karunaadhaara
Ghanataa
Mahima neeke
Sthuti aaraadhana neeke
Ghanataa mahima neeke
Ihaparamulalo neeke
||maa yesu||

Verse 1:
1. Rakshinchaavu vidipinchaavu
Akshaya krupanichi nadipinchaavu
Poshinchaavu kshemamichaavu
Rakshakudaa nee raajyamichaavu
||maa yesu||

Verse 2:
2. Adviteeyaa aadidevaa
Aatmeeya sahavaasam andinchaavu
Adbhutame aanandame
Asamaanudaa abhishekamichaavu
||maa yesu||

Verse 3:
3. Nee janulame nee swaasthyame
Nee tanuvugaa yila janminchaavu
Yaajakulame raajyameledam
Raaraajutodane ramyamugaa
||maa yesu||

Verse 4:
4. Prakatinchedam prabhupremane
Paraloka aanandam panchedamu
Prabhuve maargam prabhuve satyam
Prabhuve jeevam ani chaatedam
||maa yesu||
