Je. devaraaju
Schale bm
Yesayyaa premaamayaa ereeti sthutiyintu neekemi chellintu
Parama tandri paavanaatma vandana stutulanduko

Verse 1:
1. Aa niseedharaatrilo getsemane totalo
Marana vedana baadhalandu alasipotivaa naa prabhu
||yesayyaa||

Verse 2:
2. Kathina yudulu battiri akata hinsalu bettiri
Nepamulenno neramento mopi neepai umisiraa
||yesayyaa||

Verse 3:
3. Karaku koradaa debbalu padenu vadi nee menupai
Cheelche deham chinde raktam maunivaitivaa naa prabhu
||yesayyaa||

Verse 4:
4. Needu siluva maranamandu neevu kaarchina raktamandu
Naadu paapamu pariharinchi nannu dhanyuni jesinaavu
||yesayyaa||
