Chorus:
**
**
Daa. vai. baabjee
Schale g
Jayamani paadedamu yesuni chaatedamu
Lokamunantatini premato gelichedamu
Yese kreestani, kreeste prabhuvani paapula rakshakudani
||jaya||

Verse 1:
1. Nararupadhaariyai bhuvi nudayinchenu
Narulanu rakshimpa maranamu nondenu
Mudava dinamuna mrutyunjayudaayen
Mudamuna paadedamu halleluyageetamul
||jaya||

Verse 2:
2. Nutana srushtigaa manalanu chesenu
Nutana geetamun mana nota nunchenu
Parisuddhaatmuni varamuga nosagenu
Paadi pogededamu prabhuvunu manasaara
||jaya||

Verse 3:
3. Subhaprada nireekshana mari manakosagenu
Subhavaarta prakatinche sishyuluga chesenu
Abhayapradaatayai manato nundenu
Anisamu pogadedamu aramarikalu leka
||jaya||
