Ji. maanikyaraavu
Schale dm
Preminchi pujinchedam
Keertinchi ghanaparachedam
Sthutiyintum nee naamam
Prakatintum nee satyam
Krupanondi balamondi pani chesedam

Verse 1:
1. Maa sailambu srungambu neevekadaa
Maakedembu durgambu neevekadaa
Maa jeevitam neekankitam
Nee chentane maa aasrayam
Nee tone nee baatane nadichedam
||preminchi||

Verse 2:
2. Nee vaakyambu deepambu nadichenduku
Nee satyambu maargambu telipenduku
Pathiyinchedam prachurinchedam
Dhyaaninchedam vyaakhyaaninchedam
Nee vaakya maadhuryamunu groledam
||preminchi||

Verse 3:
3. Nee vichina panini chepattedam
Nee kichina maata neraverchedam
Bodhinchedam balaparachedam
Balamondedam sthiraparachedam
Nee divya paricharya saaginchedam
||preminchi||
