Niratam neeke sthuti stotramul
Yesu naa raaja naa devaa
Satatam neeke ghanataa mahima
Prabhaava maaropintun

Verse 1:
1. Nee prema naakento madhuram
Naa praanamuna kento modam
Edabaayani prema kadu dayato brova
Adugaduguna ninnu aaraadhintun
||niratam||

Verse 2:
2. Nanu moyuvaadavu neeve
Nanu kaayu vaadavu neeve
Naa bhaaram mosi teerambu jerchi
Nanu aadarinchu naa sarvam neeve
||niratam||

Verse 3:
3. Naa gnaanam naa neeti neeve
Parisuddhata naa vidudala neeve
Naa jeevadaataa naa praanapriyudaa
Naa atisaya kaaranamu ee jagatilo neeve
||niratam||

Verse 4:
4. Naa kaapari naa upiri neeve
Naa gaanam naa geetam neeve
Naa srutiyu neeve naa gatiyu neeve
Naa sthuti aaraadhana kaadhaaram neeve
