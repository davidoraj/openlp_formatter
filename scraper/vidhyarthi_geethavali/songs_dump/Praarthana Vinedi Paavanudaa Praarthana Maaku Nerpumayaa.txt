Praardhana vinedi paavanudaa praardhana maaku nerpumayaa
||
Praardhana||

Verse 1:
1. Sreshthamaina bhaavamu gurchi sishya brundamuku nerpitivi
Paramuda ninnu pranutinchedamu paraloka praarthana nerpumayaa
||praardhana||

Verse 2:
2. Parama devudavani telisi karamuletti jantaga modchi
Siramunu vanchi sariganu vedina sunkari praardhana nerpumayaa
||praardhana||

Verse 3:
3. Dinamulona jesina seva daivachittamuku saripova
Deenudavayi ontiga kondanu chesina praarthana nerpumayaa
||praardhana||

Verse 4:
4. Satru muka neenu chuttukoni silavapaina ninu jampaganu
Saantamuto nee satruvula brovaga salipina praardhana nerpumayaa
||praardhana||
