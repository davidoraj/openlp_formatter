Chorus:
**
**
Ke. je. yas. baaburaavu
Schale bm
Prabhuvaa prabhuvaa prabhuvaa
Parisuddha talampulanu pavitramaina manasunu
Nishkalankamaina premanu nirdosha palukula naalukanu
Naakanugrahinchumaa naakanugrahinchumaa
||prabhuvaa||

Verse 1:
1. Sareeraasayu netraasayu
Jeevapu dambamunu dushta hrudayaalochanalan
Naalo nundi tolaginchumaa
Naalo neeve nivasinchumaa
||prabhuvaa||

Verse 2:
2. Talaantulatonu buddhi gnaanamulatonu
Uppongakundunatlu atisayinchakundunatlu
Naalo neevu jeevinchumaa
Naalo neeve nivasinchumaa
||prabhuvaa||

Verse 3:
3. Nee chittamu neraverchuko tandree
Ayattaparachumu nanu devaa
Nee aagnala nadachutaku
Nanu balaparachumo devaa
||prabhuvaa||
