Chorus:
**
**
Reva. di. je. agastin
Schale ch
Yesukreestu ninnu pilichenugaa
Raavela o yuvakaa, raavela o yuvati

Verse 1:
1. Nee yavvana kaalamande prabhu kodini moyutache
Melondeda neevipudu jaagelanu yuvajanamaa
Nee
Hrudayamu prabhukimmu prabhujeevamu needagunu
||yesu||

Verse 2:
2. Gurileni nee bratuku erayaaye virodhiki
Tvarapadakunte neeku mari tappadu narakaagni
||yesu||

Verse 3:
3. Nee dehamu devunidi suddhaatmaku nilayamidi
Prabhu karpana cheyanide padagottunu apavaadi
||yesu||

Verse 4:
4. Tana vanamulo panicheya panivaaralu kaavalenu
Vinalevaa prabhu swaramu kanalevaa prabhu polamu
||yesu||
