Chorus:
**
**
Je. devaraaju
Schale e
Yesu nee naamame madhuram
Paapa vinaasanam
Saapa vimochaname gadaa
Yesu nee naamame madhuram
Halleluyaa naa devaa
Sthutigeetam nepaadedan
Halleluyaa maa devaa
Sthutiyintun nee naamamun

Verse 1:
1. Naamamulannita unnata naamam
Jeevamuniche sannuta naamam
Premaku rupam sreeyesu naamam
Nee naamame naa jeevanaraagam
||yesu||

Verse 2:
2. Patitula paalita varaprada naamam
Atulita saukhyam bosagedi naamam
Parisuddhulu ila koniyaadu naamam
Paramuna dutalu pujinchu naamam
||yesu||

Verse 3:
3. Adbhuta swasthata nichedi naamam gaaru paada
Vistruta paapam kadigedi naamam
Praarthana phalamula kaarana naamam
Aaraadhanake aadhaara naamam
||yesu||

Verse 4:
4. Cheekati saktula jayinchu naamam
Lokapu chetunu tolaginchu naamam
Jeevapu maargam bodhinchu naamam
Paramuku cherche paavana naamam
||yesu||
