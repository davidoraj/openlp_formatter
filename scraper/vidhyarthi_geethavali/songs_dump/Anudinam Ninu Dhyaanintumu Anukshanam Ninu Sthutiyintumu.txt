Chorus:
**
**
Ji. maanikyaraavu
Schale dm
Anudinam ninu dhyaanintumu
Anukshanam ninu sthutiyintumu
Neeve andagaa nee todundagaa
Preminchi praardhinchi pani chesedam
||anudinam||

Verse 1:
1. Prathamamu prabhuvani prasthutin chesedam
Nee paripurna parisuddha maargam
Payaninchute maaku jeevam
||anudinam||

Verse 2:
2. Vaakyamu dhyaanamu maruvaka chesedam
Nee divya madhurampu vaakyam
Dhyaaninchute goppa bhaagyam
||anudinam||

Verse 3:
3. Prabhupani cheyuchu rayamuna velledam
Nee suvaarta chaatintum
Nityam prakatinchi nee prema satyam
||anudinam||

Verse 4:
4. Nasinchu aatmalan viduvaka gelichedam
Nee krupacheta karuninchi mammu
Nee paricharyane cheyanimmu

Verse 5:
5. Sramalalo manasulo santasam pondedam
Aa daatrutva paricharyan memu
Saagimpa saamardhya mimmu ||anudinam||
