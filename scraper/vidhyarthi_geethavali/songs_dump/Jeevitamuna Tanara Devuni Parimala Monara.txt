Jeevitamuna tanara devuni parimala monara
Raave leve kraistavamaa yuvataramaa

Verse 1:
1. Yodhuni vale nilichi sodhanalanu gelichi
Rakshana jaya sankhaa rahi mrosi paadumaa
Sthuti geeti subhamati
||sugama||

Verse 2:
2. Devuni pilupu sumaa neevika lobadumaa
Aatmala rakshanakai asrulato premato
Praarthanato pranamilu
||vyadhita||

Verse 3:
3. Yauvana jeevaname aa prabhundolikinche
Yauvana kraistavamaa devunito saagipo
Siluvaye nee pataaka
||sidhila||

Verse 4:
4. Tejoyudhamulane divyaatmuni krupane
Dhariyinchumu nede varadusi niluvumaa
Khalamatine geluvumaa
||dalita||
