Je. devaraaju
Schale ch
Sreeyesuto oka chinnamaata
Chikkulu deerchunu unnamaata
Aasalanniyu neraverun
Akkara deerchunu yesubaata

Verse 1:
1. Niraasapadaku nisprahavaladu
Paraaku maani praarthinchumu
Parama prabhu karuninchunu
Nirantara krupache balamondumu
||sreeyesuto||

Verse 2:
2. Viduvaka vedi visugunu maani
Adigina vaariki dorukunugaa
Kadudayato kanikarinchun
Tadabada vaddika sthirapadumu
||sreeyesuto||

Verse 3:
3. Rottenadigite raatinivvadu
Tattina vaariki terachunugaa
Pattuga neevu praarthinchagaa
Dattamugaa krupa aavarinchugaa
||sreeyesuto||
