Je. devaraaju
Schale g
Cheri kolvudee sthutinchudee sreeyesuni
Koricherudi arpinchudee krutagnatal
Sarva bhu prajalaaraa sarva srushti jeevulaaraa (2)

Verse 1:
1. Aascharyakarudani aalochana kartani
Nityudaina tandrani samaadhaanakarta prabhuvani
||cheri||

Verse 2:
2. Paapasaapaharudani paapikaasrayambani
Paramadaiva narudani paavanaatma purna priyudani
||cheri||

Verse 3:
3. Ennadaina maarani yesu naamadhaaruni
Ennadu edabaayani immaanuyelu devuni
||cheri||

Verse 4:
4. Raajulake raajani prabhulake prabhudani
Aalphaa omegani aadi deva tejo rupani
||cheri||
