Ji. maanikyaraavu
Schale em
Tandriki priyamaina pillalam
Munduku saagi velledam
Devuniki nija daasulam
Chetulu patti saagedam
Balame balame nee chetilo
Jayame jayame nee needalo
Balame jayame nee baatalo

Verse 1:
1. Praakaaram padi yunden
Nirmaanam mana kartavyam
Praardhinchudaam payaninchudaam
Panichesi niluva bettedam
||balame||

Verse 2:
2. Kaalejee mana kota polam
Vidyaardhi viluvaina phalam
Adugu pettedam adigi chuchedam
Aatmapanta panalu kosedam
||balame||

Verse 3:
3. Raarandi cherandi
Sahakaaram andinchandi
Vichaarinchudaam vivarinchudaam
Samakurchi balaparachedam
||balame||
