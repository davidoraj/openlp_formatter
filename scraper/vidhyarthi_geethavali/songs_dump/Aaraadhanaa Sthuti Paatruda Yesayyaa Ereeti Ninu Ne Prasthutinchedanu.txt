Aaraadhanaa sthuti paatruda yesayyaa ereeti ninu ne prasthutinchedanu
Neeraajanamulato needu preman niratamu sthutincheda nee naamamun

Verse 1:
1. Naa paapa bhaaram siluvalo mosi vidudala nichitive
Naa saapamanta tolaginchitivi suddhuni jesitive
Rakshana neeve raajyamu neede prabhuvaa devaa nee jaali krupakai
Niratamu sthutincheda nee naamamun
||aaraadhanaa||

Verse 2:
2. Nutana jeevam nutana balamu nutana manasichaave
Anudina nutana vaatsalyamuto dinadinamu karuninchaave
Jeevamu neeve jayamuneeve devuni tanayaa neetyaagam korakai
Niratamu sthutincheda neenaamamun
||aaraadhanaa||

Verse 3:
3. Saataanu kriyalanni layaparichaavu cheekati tolaginchaave
Maranapu mullunu virichaavu neevu kaadini khandinchaave
Vaakyamu neeve jayamu neede devuni tanayaa neetyaagam korakai
Niratamu sthutincheda neenaamamun
||aaraadhanaa||

Verse 4:
4. Aatmato nimpi aakali teerchi aadaranichitive
Abhishekamichi aasanu teerchi aatmala nichitive
Aatmayuneeve satyamu neeve aatmala kaapari neekaapudalakai
Niratamu sthutincheda nee naamamun
||aaraadhana||
