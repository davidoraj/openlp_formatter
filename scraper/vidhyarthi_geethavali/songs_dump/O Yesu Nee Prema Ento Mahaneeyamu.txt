Chorus:
**
**
Ti. di. maathyus
Schale ch
O yesu nee prema ento mahaneeyamu
Aakaasataara parvata samudramulakannaa goppadi

Verse 1:
1. Agamya aanandame hrudayamu nindenu
Prabhuni kaaryamulu gambheeramainavi
Prati udaya saayantramulu sthutiki yogyamulu
||o yesu||

Verse 2:
2. Sankata samayamulo saagalekunnaanu
Daya chupu naameeda ani nenu morapettagaa
Vintinantini naa moraku munde todanundunantivi
||o yesu||

Verse 3:
3. Devuni gruhamulo sadaa sthutinchedanu
Sampurna hrudayamuto sadaa bhajinchedanu
Sthuti prasansalaku yogyudesu halleluya aamen
||o yesu||
