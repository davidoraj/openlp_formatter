Verse 2:
2. Nee draakshatotalo panichesi
Nee prema baatalo vembadinchi
Nee vaarta chaatuchu parigetti
Nee sthuti paatato jayinchedam
||
Aaraadhinchi||

Verse 3:
3. Praardhinchi poraadi gelichedamu
Vignaapanaatmato vimalaatmato
Needu sankalpam neravera
Nee hastam kadili panicheyagaa
||
Aaraadhinchi||
