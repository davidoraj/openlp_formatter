Nilakadagaa konasaagedamaa
Parisuddha lekhanamulayandu
Alayaka anubhava gnaanamuto
Paluvidhamula phaliyinchedamaa

Verse 1:
1. Aatma preritamu daivaatma sakti puritamu
Nityamu velugunu prasarinchun ila
Satyapu baatalo nadipinchun
||nilakadagaa||

Verse 2:
2. Aadiyandali vaakyam sareeradhaariga venchesen
Aatmakhadgamu vibhajinchun yika
Apavaadini edirinchedamaa
||nilakadagaa||

Verse 3:
3. Upadesinchedamu khandinchi tappunu savarinchi
Apavitratanu vidanaade nija
Naitika viluvalu nerpedamaa
||nilakadagaa||

Verse 4:
4. Vinutaku vegiramu angeekarinchedi saatvikamu
Anudina vaakyapu aacharane mana
Dhanyata bhaagyam ee dharalo
||nilakadagaa||
