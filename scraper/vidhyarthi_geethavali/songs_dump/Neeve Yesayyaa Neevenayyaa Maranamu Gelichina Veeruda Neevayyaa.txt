Neeve yesayyaa neevenayyaa
Maranamu gelichina veeruda neevayyaa

Verse 1:
1. Punarut'thaana balamu neeve
Maranapu baluni gulchi gelichaavu neeve
Maranapu mullunu virichaavu neeve
Maranapu daasyapu bhayamunu baapitivi
||neeve||

Verse 2:
2. Marana balamunu vyardhamu chesi
Veliginchitivi jeevaakshayatal
Marana sareeraramu mahimanu daalchaga
Mahimaiswaryamu manujulakichitivi
||neeve||

Verse 3:
3. Akshaya jeevam andinchaavu
Anudina jeevita jayamichaavu
Paapapu niyamamu tolaginchaavu
Aatma niyamamuto mamu karuninchitivi
||neeve||
