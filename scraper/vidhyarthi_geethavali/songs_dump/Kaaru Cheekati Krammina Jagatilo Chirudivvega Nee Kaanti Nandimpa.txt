Chorus:
**
**
Ji. eph. vi. prasaad
Schale g
Kaaru cheekati krammina jagatilo
Chirudivvega nee kaanti nandimpa
Varamidumo prabhu ee tarunamulo

Verse 1:
1. Loka samaapti suchana livigo
Kadanamulunu bhukampamulu
Dveshamuto nindina nagni jvaalalu
Niseedhi kriyale nikhila jagatilo
||kaaru||

Verse 2:
2. E ruchileni jeevitamulaku
Nee ruchinivvaga nerpumu niratamu
Duramanakanu bhaaramanakanu
Vegame chaateda chirajeeva vaarta
||kaaru||

Verse 3:
3. Narakamu loniki joge vaariki
Telupaga nerpumu paraloka vaarta
Cheekatito nindina manasulalo
Nimpaga nerpumu nee divya kaanti
||kaaru||
