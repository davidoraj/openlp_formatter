Prabhuvaa Sahaayamu Nimmu Vibhudaa Nee Sannidhi Nimmu
https://www.christiandatahouse.com/2023/02/prabhuva-sahayamu-nimmu-vibhuda-nee-sannidhi-nimmu.html
Chorus:
**
**
Ke. je. yas baaburaavu
Schale em
Prabhuvaa, sahaayamu nimmu, vibhudaa nee sannidhi nimmu
Maato naduvumaa, mammu nadupumaa ellavelala
||prabhuvaa||

Verse 1:
1. Lokamantaa cheekatimayamu, maadu bratukila bhaarabharitam
Sakalamu sokamayamu, vikalamaaye maadu hrudayam
Sakalamu sokamayamu, aadukonumu mammu devaa
||prabhuvaa||

Verse 2:
2. Maadu bratukulo balaheenulamu, cheduveru molachuno emo
Anisamu kaachi, brochi jaagarukata nimmu devaa
||prabhuvaa||

Verse 3:
3. Maadu bodhalo doshamuleka, veshadhaarana asale leka
Satyamun swechhagaanu teliyaparachi dhairyamimmu
||prabhuvaa||
