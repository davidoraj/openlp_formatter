Je. devaraaju
Schale ch
Ghanadaiva diviteja darsinchumu mahimaatmato mamu nimpumu
Yesuraajaa maa neetiraajaa

Verse 1:
1. Saakshyajeevitamu sannagillipoye
Sahakaara sahavaasam prema heenamayye
Mamu karuninchi nutana parachumu
||ghana||

Verse 2:
2. Praarthana phalamulu pondaka potimi
Nee sevayanduna somarulamaitimi
Aatmala rakshimpanee saktineeyumaa
||ghana||

Verse 3:
3. Needu chittamu cheya nerpinchumayyaa
Ninu vembadimpaga velugimmayyaa
Aasato nee dari jeritimayyaa
||ghana||

Verse 4:
4. Maa yuvataramantaa paadaipoye
Saanti samaadhaanam karuvaipoye
Prakatinchedamu nee prema suvaarta
||ghana||
