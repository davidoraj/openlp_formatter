Chorus:

Je. devaraaju
Schale d
Prati udayamuna sadayuni cheri
Praarthinchi prabhuni sthutiyinchumu
Satatamu neevu morrapettumu
Paraloka tandri aalakinchunu
Saranaarthula nella karuninchunu badulichunu
||prati udayamuna||

Verse 1:
1. Praatakaala praarthanalu
Dinamella ninnu nadipinchunu
Aatmato chesina praarthanale
Neeti kavachamai nannu kaayunu
||prati udayamuna||

Verse 2:
2. Vekuva jaamuna nee swaramu
Vinabadanimmu prabhuvunaku
Anudina krupanu neekandinchi
Vinipinchu neeku tana swaramu
||prati udayamuna||

Verse 3:
3. Paraloka mannaa prati udayam
Saripadu nantaga kurchukonumu
Karunato prabhuve samakurchunugaa
Aranyapu bhojana ballayunu
||prati udayamuna||
