Aadyanta rahitudaa aascharyarupudaa
Ar'hudaa yogyudaa paatrudaa
Anduko neeke maa aaraadhana

Verse 1:
1. Neevegaa srushtinchi nirminchaavu
Nee praanam arpinchi rakshinchaavu
Nee krupa chaalayyaa neepreme balamayyaa
Nee chittam maalo neraveraalayyaa
||aadyanta||

Verse 2:
2. Nee swaasdhyam maakichi poshinchaavu
Nee vaakyam maakichi nadipinchaavu
Nee sannidhi chaalayyaa nee aatme balamayyaa
Nee rupam maalo rupondaalayyaa
||aadyanta||

Verse 3:
3. Nee raajyam maakichi vidipinchaavu
Nee sevalo balamichi phalamichaavu
Nee jeevanchaalayyaa neevarame chaalayyaa
Nee sangham nityam vardhillaalayyaa
||aadyanta||
