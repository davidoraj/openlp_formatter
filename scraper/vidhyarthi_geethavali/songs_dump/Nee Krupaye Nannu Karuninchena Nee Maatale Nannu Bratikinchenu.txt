Nee krupaye nannu karuninchenu

Yesayyaa. . . . naa mes'seeyaa

Verse 1:
1. Kashtamulo naadu kanneeru tudichaavu
Dhairyamichaavu neeve kadaa (2)
Kalata sramayainanu satru pannaagamu (2)
Tolaga chesaavu neeve kadaa
||nee krupaye||

Verse 2:
2. Naaku sahaayam naadurgam naagaanam
Rakshanaadhaaram neeve kadaa
Saahasa kaaryamu kannula kaascharyamu
Chesina hastamu needekadaa
||nee krupaye||

Verse 3:
3. Neeve devudavu krupaadayaaludavu
Krutagnataastutulu chellintunu
Ninnu ghanaparatunu nee kriyalu vivarintunu
Krutagnataarpanalu arpintunu
||nee krupaye||
