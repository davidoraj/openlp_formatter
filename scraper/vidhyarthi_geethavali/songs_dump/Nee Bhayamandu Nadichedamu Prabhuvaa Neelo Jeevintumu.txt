Schale f#m
Nee bhayamandu nadichedamu, prabhuvaa neelo jeevintumu
Ee bhuviyandunnantavaraku, nee diviyandu cheruvaraku

Verse 1:
1. Yudaya, galilaya, samaraya desamula
Nee dayan sanghamu kshemamu ponduchu
Nee dari samaadhaanamu ponde
Aadarinchumu mammu kudaa devaa
||nee bhaya||

Verse 2:
2. Yosepu daasundainanu aiguptulo
Vaasiga nee bhayamanduna bhaasille
Daasudu kaaledu paapamuku
Yesayyaa mamu balaparachumayaa
||nee bhaya||

Verse 3:
3. Lokamu, sareeramu, saataanun lekkacheyaka
Sokamu, aihika baadhato krungipoka
Aakasamandali ninu chera
Nee krupa nimmayaa priya yesayyaa
||nee bhaya||
