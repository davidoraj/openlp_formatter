Je. devaraaju
Schale d
Yesuni gamyam cheraali jeevita viluvalalo
Yese gamyam kaavaali jeevita baatalalo
Guriyoddake parigettudaam
Sampurnulamaudaam
Venukativi marachedamu
Munduku velledamu (2)

Verse 1:
1. Bratukuta kreestese bratukuta prabhu korake
Balaparachunu yese bhariyinchunu yese
Bahumaanam yese
Halleluya paatalu paadi prabhu nalarinchedamaa
Halleluya sthuti paadi prabhu nutiyinchedamaa
||halleluya||

Verse 2:
2. Siluvanu moyudumaa khalunila gelichedamaa
Prabhunila chaatedamaa sthiramuga nilichedamaa
Prabhukai vechedamaa
||halleluya||

Verse 3:
3. Sahavaasamulona sahakaaramutoda
Saakshi kutumbamugaa premalo nedugudumaa
Aikyata korudumaa
||halleluya||
