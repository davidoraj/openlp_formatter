Nee vaakyamu naa paadamulaku deepamu deepamu
Naa trovaku velugai velugaiyunnatti aa deepamu

Verse 1:
1. Maarani vaakyamu aarani deepamu anaadi devuni vaagdaanamu
Halleluya halleluya naathudesudu kreestu raajuku halleluya

Verse 2:
2. Nee dharmasaastramu naakento priyamu
Nee nyaaya vidhulu naakaahaaram
||halleluya||

Verse 3:
3. Juntiteniyala vanti pravaaham
Kante mari kammani minti vaakyam
||halleluya||

Verse 4:
4. Nararupamettina paraloka vaakyamaa
Naato maatlaadumaa naa prabhuvaa
||halleluya||

Verse 5:
5. Bangaaru kantenu aparanji kantenu
Nee aagnalanduna adhika vaanchha
||halleluya||
