Je. devaraaju
Schale em
Mahimaanvita yesu mahaaghanudaa
Manujaavataaraa manoharudaa
Maraninchitivi mrutyunjayudaa
Maaranaata neeke stotram prabhuvaa

Verse 1:
1. Naa aatmalo balamu naa paatalo madhuram
Naa maatalo jeevam neevenayyaa
Nee prema nichi nanu karuninchitivi
Nee rakta kraya dhanamuto rakshinchitivi
Parisuddha parachi nee prema nimpi
Nee sottuga nanu jesi deevinchitive

Verse 2:
2. Nindanu tolaginchi bandhaalu tempi
Dendamulo nee andam ponduga nimpi
Krupaa mahadaiswaryam andinchitivi
Nindaarahitunigaa nanu karuninchitivi
Nirdoshamaargamu nutanamagu hrudayamu
Ninu nenu sevimpa erparachitive
