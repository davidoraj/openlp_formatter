Sainyamula kadhipatiyagu devaa
Nee nivaasamulu enta ramyamulu

Verse 1:
1. Needu mandira aavaranam chudavalenani naa hrudayam
Entayo aasato sommasillenayaa sommasillenayaa
||sainyamula||

Verse 2:
2. Needu mandiramulalona dhanyule nivasinchedaru
Nityamu vaaralu nin nutinchedaru nin nutinchedaru
||sainyamula||

Verse 3:
3. Bhakti heena gudaaramulo nenu nivasinchutakante
Undute mechudune needu dvaaramuna needu dvaaramuna
||sainyamula||
