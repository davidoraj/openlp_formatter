Naa Yesu Prema Ento Madhuram

Chorus:
**
**
Ke. je. yas. baaburaavu
Schale em
Naa yesu prema ento madhuram
Naa yesu prema ento balamu
Emani nenu vivarinchagalanu, entani nenu ilaa paadagalanu
||naa yesu||

Verse 1:
1. Kaluvari giripaini sramalanu chuda
Siluvalo naa yesu premento madhuram
Tuluvalu kottinaa prema chupene
Mulukulu gruchinaa preminchene
||naa yesu||

Verse 2:
2. Talachina naa yesu premanu nenu
Viluvaina prema adi ento ghanamu
Ilalona e prema saati raadule
Kalalonaina maruvalenule
||naa yesu||

Verse 3:
3. Talachina pilichina palikedi prema
Nalatalu kalatalu tolaginchu prema
Malachenu naa bratukunu ati madhuramugaa
Pilichenu nannilalo tana sishyunigaa
||naa yesu||
