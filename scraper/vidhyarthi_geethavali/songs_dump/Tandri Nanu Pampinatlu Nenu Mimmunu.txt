Verse 1:
1. Kota vistaaramu koyuvaaru kondare
Kontayaina cheyabuni kalasi saagudi

Verse 2:
2. Polamu parikinchudi pairu kuda siddhame
Panalu vangi tellabaari nelakorigenu

Verse 3:
3. Saakshulai saagudi siluva saktito seeghrame
Satyavaartanu sambhramamuto chaati cheppudi

Verse 4:
4. Sishyulunu cheyudi sarva srushti nantatini
Sarvakaalamu sarvavelala meeku todundunu
