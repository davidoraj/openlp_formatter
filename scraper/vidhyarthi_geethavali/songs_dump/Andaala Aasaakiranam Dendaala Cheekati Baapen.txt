Andaala aasaakiranam dendaala cheekati baapen
Udayinche chudu sadayundu yesu nija santasa krismas nedu

Verse 1:
1. Yuvatara bhavitavyam yese navatara chaitanyam yese
Prabhavinchenu diviraajyam dharalo prabhuvichenu subhasandesam
||andaala||

Verse 2:
2. Darileni duraalalo gurileni valayaalalo
Tirugaadu maanavulakutodai bhariyinchun palu bhaaramulan
||andaala||

Verse 3:
3. Arunodaya darsanamidiye asamaana tejomayude
Arudenchenu aascharyakarudai andinchenu adbhuta preman
||andaala||
