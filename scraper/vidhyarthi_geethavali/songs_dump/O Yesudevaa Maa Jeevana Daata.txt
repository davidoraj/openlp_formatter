O Yesudevaa Maa Jeevana Daata
https://www.christiandatahouse.com/2023/02/oh-yesudeva-maa-jeevana-daatha.html
Chorus:
**
**
Yam. krishtaya
Schale ch
O yesudevaa maa jeevana daata
Mammu neelo nilupumu verutanni meedikedigi
Aatma devunilo phaliyinchutakai

Verse 1:
1. Nee draakshavalli kaarudraakshalu kaayaga
Neeve nijamaina draakshavallipai
Nee loni teegaluga mamu maarchitivi
||o yesu||

Verse 2:
2. Nee yandu memundi nee vaakyam maalo nundagaa
Memu bahugaa neelo edigedamu
Aatmaphalamulato phaliyinchutakai
||o yesu||

Verse 3:
3. Nee jeeva vaakyamuto nee prema vaarta chaataga
Nee aatmato nimpi mammu nadupumu devaa
Nasiyinchu vidyaarthula rakshana korakai
||o yesu||

Verse 4:
4. Nee prema nadipimpu intavarakichina prabhuvani
Anudinamu ninne memu sthutiyintumu
Maatodu needa maa sarvamani
||o yesu||
