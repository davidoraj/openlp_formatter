Chorus:
**
**
Je. devaraaju
Schale am
Yesu priyudaa aatmanaadhudaa
Aasrayintumu needu needanu
Nee krupa chaalunu premamayudaa
Maa stutulanduko daiva tanayudaa

Verse 1:
1. Maa bahumaanam maa kedemu neeve
Aasraya durgam aadhaarameeve
Maa kondayu kotayu neeve
Maa velugunu vimochanam neeve
||yesu||

Verse 2:
2. Rakshana kedem andinchu vaadavu
Dakshina hastam aadukonunu
Hechinchenu nee saatvikamu
Maa chotunu visaala parachitivi
||yesu||

Verse 3:
3. Maa deepamunu veliginchuvaadavu
Cheekati velugugaa maarchuvaadavu
Nee balamun dharimpajetuvu
Nee maargamul bayalu parutuvu
||yesu||
