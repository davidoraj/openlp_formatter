Chorus:
**
**
Je. devaraaju
Schale em
Yesunaadhuni chenta cherumu
Nee duranta enta paapamanta povunu
Yesunaadhuni cheri vedumu
Needu hrudayamanta yesu saanti nindunu
Yese maargam satyam jeevam
Raavaa sodaraa sodaree jaalamela raa

Verse 1:
1. Siluvamraanipai yesu vrelaaden
Sakala loka paapamanta pariharinchenu
Siluva needalo yesu todugaa
Avadhileni aa prema ninnu piluvagaa
||raavaa||

Verse 2:
2. Yesu premalo sedadeerumu (2)
Alasinatti needumadi visraanti nondunu (2)
Yesu premalo nilichi yundumu
Aa prema neelo prabhaavinchagaa
||raavaa||

Verse 3:
3. Yesu velugunu vembadinchumu
Yesu baatalo cheekatasale yundadu
Yesu velugulo nadichi vellumu
Aa velugu neelo prakaasinchagaa
||raavaa||

Verse 4:
4. Parama santasam nityajeevame pana
Hrudaya bhaaramanta poyi haayi nindunu (2)
Yesu baasalo santasinchumu
Dendamaanandame pongiporalagaa
||raavaa||
