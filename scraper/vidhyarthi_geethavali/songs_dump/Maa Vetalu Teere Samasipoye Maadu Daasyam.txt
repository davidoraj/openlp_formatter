Taalluri prasaad
Schale em
Maa vetalu teere samasipoye maadu daasyam
Maa eduta niliche sudhalu kurise kottalokam
Apajayame ledule bratukantaa kalimile
Gata vyadhalannee marugaipoyele

Verse 1:
1. Gatinchele maadu saapam saapam
Jeevitame kotta rupam rupam
Vedana rodana vinnapaale devuni mungita moralaaye (2)
Yehovaa bala prabhaavaale kadali cheelchi nadipinchele

Verse 2:
2. Aranyame maaku maargam maargam
Bandalu teerchunu maadu daaham daaham
Paalu tenelu pravahinchede memadugide aa desam
Viduvadu manalanennatikee
Prabhuve daivam manakandarikee

Verse 3:
3. Sthutintumu prabhuni naamam naamam
Elolam maadu jeevam jeevam
Ennadu ikapai tolagipomu devuni memu vidichipomu
Aayane maa sarvaswam aayanake bratukankitam
