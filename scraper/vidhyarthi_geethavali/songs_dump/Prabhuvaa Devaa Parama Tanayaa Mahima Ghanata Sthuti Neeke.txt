Verse 1:
1. Krupato brochitivi kanikaramuto mamu penchitivi
Nepamul trositivi neramulanu kshamiyinchitivi
Daakshinya purnudaa karunaaseeludaa (2)
Akshaya jeevamaa saranaagatudavu
||prabhuvaa||

Verse 2:
2. Raktam arpinchi raajyamugaa mamu chesitivi
Jeevam andinchi yaajakuluga mamu chesitivi
Saktiyu balamunu aiswarya gnaanamul
Neeke chellunu gottepilla devudaa
||prabhuvaa||
