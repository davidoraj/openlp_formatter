2. Adhikulamani uppongaka alpulamani madi krungaka
Andarito sahavaasam
Andarito samaadhaanam (2)

Verse 3:
3. Tanapara bhedamu vidichedamu tanuvunu prabhukarpinchedamu
Mana korakai prabhuvachen
Mana korakai prabhuvichen (2)
