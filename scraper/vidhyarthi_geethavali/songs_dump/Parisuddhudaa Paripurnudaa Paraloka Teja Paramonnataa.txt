Je. devaraaju
Schale em
Parisuddhudaa paripurnudaa
Paraloka teja paramonnataa
Yesu maa priyudaa
Prabhuvaa parisuddha phalamula nimmudevaa

Verse 1:
1. Nee arpananto parisuddhamu
Nee jeevavaakyam parisuddhamu
Nee raktadhaaralu parisuddhamu
Nee aatmanaamam parisuddhamu (2)
||parisuddhudaa||

Verse 2:
2. Parisuddha piluputo pilichitivi
Parisuddhulanugaa teerchitivi
Parisuddha phalamulu koritivi
Parisuddha varamulanichitivi (2)
||parisuddhudaa||

Verse 3:
3. Sareera kaaryamula pavitramul cheya'uru.
Sareemento balaheenamu
Sareerame nee aalayamu
Sareerame sajeevayaagamu (2)
||parisuddhudaa||

Verse 4:
4. Parisuddhulamugaa jeevintumu
Parisuddha saakshyamu nilipedamu
Parisuddha swaasthyamu pondedamu
Parisuddha kreestuni chupedamu (2)
||parisuddhudaa||
