Chorus:
**
**
Je. devaraaju
Schale em
Sthutiki paatrudaa deva sutuda maa prabhu
Hita dayaaludaa raaja nuti narpintumu
Kshitilo mammu brochiti preeti toda vaatsalyam chupitivi
Nityamu needu premakai satyamuto stotrintumu

Verse 1:
1. Innaallu poshinchitivi unnaanani neevantivi devaa
Nindaara deevinchitivi mendaina nee dayato
Samruddhi neevegaa sarvaadhikaarivi
Sajeevudaa devaa maa deena stutulanduko
||sthutiki||

Verse 2:
2. Maa morala naalinchitivi maakaramula balaparachiti veevu
Varamulato mamu nimpitivi varadaata maa prabhu
Samruddhi neevegaa sarvaadhikaarivi
Sajeevudaa devaa maa deena stutulanduko
||sthutiki||

Verse 3:
3. Maa lemilo sampada neeve nispruhalo nireekshana neeve gata
Baadhalalo nemmadi neeve maa gaadhala antam neeve
Samruddhi neevegaa sarvaadhikaarivi
Sajeevudaa devaa maa deena stutulanduko
||sthutiki||
