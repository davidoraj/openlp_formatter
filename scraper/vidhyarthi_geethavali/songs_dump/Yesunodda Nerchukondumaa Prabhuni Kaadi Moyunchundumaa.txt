Yesunodda nerchukondumaa
Prabhuni kaadi moyunchundumaa
Deenulamai naduchukondumaa
Deenamanasu kaligiyundumaa
Nerchukondumu naduchukondumu
Yesu manasu kaligiyundumu
Satyavaakyamun cheppattuchu
Kreestu sishyulamuga saagipodumaa

Verse 1:
1. Daaritappi tirugutunna
Vidyaarthula dariki cheredam
Nityajeevambu nichinaa
Yesu dariki nadipinchedam
Yesu kaadini moyudam
Premavaartanu chaatedam
Yesupaatalu paadedam
Yesubaatalo saagedam
||nerchukondumu||

Verse 2:
2. Yuddhajvaala prajvarillinaa
Penutuphaanu laavarinchinaa
Upadravam chuttimuttinaa
Utsahinchi mundukegudam
Kreestu kaadini moyudam
Siluva vaartanu chaatedam
Yesu paatalu paadedam
Yesu baatalo saagedam tataram
||nerchukondumu||
