Saranam Saranam Saranam Devaa
Song of Sol. - 5:16
Chorus:
"atadu atikaankshaneeyudu. itade naa priyudu. itade naa snehitudu. " paramageetamu song of songs 516
Pallavi saranam saranam saranam devaa karuna naathudaa
Karuna naathudaa ee taruname prabho

Verse 1:
1. Paaparahita devakumaaraa saapavaahakaa
Saapavaahakaa nitya kopa rahitudaa
|| saranam ||

Verse 2:
2. Paripurna devudaa naraavataarudaa
Naraavataarudaa maa yesu naathudaa
|| saranam ||

Verse 3:
3. Dayaamayunda kreestu yesu daakshinya prabhuvaa
Daakshinya prabhuvaa baahulya devudaa
|| saranam ||

Verse 4:
4. Nammadagina lokarakshakaa sarvopakaarudaa
Sarvopakaarudaa sarvasaktimantudaa
|| saranam ||

Verse 5:
5. Saatvikundaa sarvajanula kaankshaneeyudaa
Kaankshaneeyudaa vaatsalya devudaa
|| saranam ||

Verse 6:
6. Riktudai tagginchukonina vinayapurnudaa
Vinayapurnudaa mamugaachu devudaa
|| saranam ||

Verse 7:
7. Satyavantudavu maadu nityadevudaa
Nityadevudaa maa manchi bodhakudaa
|| saranam ||

Verse 8:
8. Sarvaloka srushtikarta satyadevudaa
Satyadevudaa maa nityajeevamaa
|| saranam ||

Verse 9:
9. Ellarilo sreshthudaa maa vallabhundavu
Vallabhundavu halleluya paadedam
|| saranam ||
