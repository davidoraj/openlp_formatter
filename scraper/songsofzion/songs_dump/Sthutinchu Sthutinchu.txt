Sthutinchu Sthutinchu
Pallavi : Stutinchu stutinchu - prabhu Yesu ne stutinchu (2)
Chorus:
"saaswatamaina premato nenu ninnu preminchuchunnaanu" yirmiyaa jeremiah 313
Pallavi sthutinchu sthutinchu prabhu yesu ne sthutinchu 2
Ninnu nirminchi rupambu nichina srushtikartaayane 2
Jeevapu daata aayane nee rakshana kartaayane 2

Verse 1:
1. Preminchenu ninnu aayane saaswata premache
Erparachu kone ninnu bhumi puttakamunupe
Devude nararupiyaaye aayana peru yesu prabhu 2 aayana
|| sthutinchu ||

Verse 2:
2. Yaakobunu srujiyinche israayeluku rupuniche
Neeku todaiyundun bhayapadakumani palike
Peru petti pilichi naa sottu neevanenu naa sottu neevanenu
|| sthutinchu ||

Verse 3:
3. Preminchi nannu rakshinche sthuti chellinchi pujintun
Tana rupamu naaku niche aaraadhinchi ghanaparatun
Paraloka pauriniga chesen hrudayaarpana narpintun
|| sthutinchu ||

Verse 4:
4. Sahavaasamulo nilichi pratyakshata yandundi
Emi sambhavinchinanu prabhu pakshamu nundavalen
Antamandu bahumaanamittun ani prabhuve cheppenugaa
|| sthutinchu ||
