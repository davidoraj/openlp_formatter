Pagatilo Megha Stambhamugaa
Din men badal ka chhaya bana
Chorus:
"yesu taane daggaraku vachi vaarito kuda nadichenu. " lukaa luke 2415
Pagatilo megha stambhamugaa
Raatrilo yagni stambhamugaa
Rakshakudavugaa nundi prabhuvaa
Sukhasaantito gaachitivi
Pallavi dhanya dhanya dhanya prabhuku
Taane rakshana mana kosage
Nee sannidhiki nadipi prabhuvaa
Raatrimbavallu gaachitivi

Verse 1:
1. Vaakyamannaato poshinchi
Banda neellanu traaginchitivi
Aatmaku trupti nichi prabhuvaa
Saantini naa kichitivi
|| dhanya ||

Verse 2:
2. Aatmeeya sundara hrudayamulo
Aatma praana sareeramulo
Aatmeeyajeeva mosagi prabhuvaa
Aatmanu melkolipitivi
|| dhanya ||

Verse 3:
3. Kaanaanuloniki techitivi
Parasudhdhulato jerchitivi
Pranutinchedanu ninnu prabhuvaa
Saagilapadi pujintun
|| dhanya ||
