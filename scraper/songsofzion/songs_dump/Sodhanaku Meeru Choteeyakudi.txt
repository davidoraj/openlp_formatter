Sodhanaku Meeru Choteeyakudi
James 4:7
Chorus:
"apavaadini edirinchudi" yaakobu james 47
Sodhanaku meeru choteeyakudi
Dhairyamu vahinchi poraatamunu
Saadhinchedu vaaru jayinchedaru
Saataanuku longabaapambagunu
Pallavi yesu saktini gori yella kaalamu vedu
Yesudaasato mimmu daasi nadupunu

Verse 1:
1. Durbuddhi kubhaasha maanundu sadaa
Paapaatmula pondu tappinchukoni
Sree devuni perun dushimpakayu
Slaaghinchu meeru varthilludilan
|| yesu ||

Verse 2:
2. Jayincheduvaaru saundarya prabhun
Vaaru makutambu dharinchedaru
Prabhundagu yesu nikkambu nammu
Daayane nityambu saayamunichun
|| yesu ||
