Aasreshthambau Granthamulo
The grand old Book
Chorus:
Aasreshthambau granthamulo
Aasreshthambau granthamulo
Echota chuchina aadaranayunnadi
Sukha duḥkhamulo vaagdaanamunnadi
Viswaasamunchi dhyaaninchudi
The grand old book (2)
You find the words of chomfort
Wherever you may look
In sorrow or in pain
His promises are plain
So keep on believing
In the grand old book
