Ee Jagatiki Jyotini Nenu
Mathew 5:14
Chorus:
"meeru lokamunaku velugai yunnaaru" mattayi matthew 514
Pallavi ee jagatiki jyotini nenu
Jeevana jyoti jvalinchedanu

Verse 1:
1. Naa gruhambunaku jyotini nenu
Naa jyotini jvalinchi sadaa
Gaadhaandhakaaramunantatini
Tolagincheda naa hrudayamunundi
|| ee jagatiki ||

Verse 2:
2. Kreestu rahitamai graamaseemalu
Kunukuchunnavi cheekatiyandu
Prabhu yesu jyotini veligincheda
Prati cheekati loya yandu
|| ee jagatiki ||

Verse 3:
3. Pranayamu tyaagamu satyamanunavi
Prabhu vosangina pratyakshata
Santoshamuna naa jeevitamu
Samarpincheda prabhu yesunaku
|| ee jagatiki ||
