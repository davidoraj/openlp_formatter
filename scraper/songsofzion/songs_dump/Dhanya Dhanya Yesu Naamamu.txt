Dhanya Dhanya Yesu Naamamu
Psalms - 72:19
Chorus:
"aayana mahimagala naamamu nityamu sthutimpabadunugaaka" keertana psalm 175
Pallavi dhanya dhanya yesu naamamu
Jayajaya prabhu naamamu

Verse 1:
1. Yesu perata mukti dorakun
Prabhunaamame suddhi parachun
|| dhanya ||

Verse 2:
2. Yesu naamame ento priyamu
Duḥkhitula kaasraya naamam
|| dhanya ||

Verse 3:
3. Ramyamainadi yesuni naamam
Manassaanti nichu naamam
|| dhanya ||

Verse 4:
4. Yesu naamame sakti kaladi
Prabhu peruna bhutamu tolagen
|| dhanya ||

Verse 5:
5. Ihaparamulalo yesu naamam
Mahonnatamaina naamam
|| dhanya ||
