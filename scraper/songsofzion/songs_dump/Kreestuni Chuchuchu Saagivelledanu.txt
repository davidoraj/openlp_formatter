Kreestuni Chuchuchu Saagivelledanu
Heb. 12:1,2
Chorus:
"inta goppa saakshi samuhamu meghamuvale manalanu aavarinchiyunnanduna manamu kuda pratibhaaramunu, suluvugaa chikkulabettu paapamunu vidichipetti, viswaasamunaku kartayu daanini konasaaginchu vaadunaina yesuvaipu chuchuchu, mana yeduta unchabadina pandemulo opikato parugettudamu" hebree hebrews 121
Kreestuni chuchuchu saagivelledanu seeyonu yaatralo
Lokamun vidichi cherukondunu mahima desamun
Kreestuni anugrahamulo surakshita hastamulalo
Mahaanandamuto sthutinchuchu cherudu naadesamun
Pallavi ento sundara jeevitamu mahimato nindinadi
Sadaa sarvadaa kreestuto nundi
Mahima parachedanu mahimalo nundedanu

Verse 1:
1. Duḥkha sramalalo nannu rakshinchu daaguchotu aayane
Sankataklesa pareekshalalo jayamu pondedanu
Prabhunikai jeevinchedanu aayana adugu jaadalalo
Aatma praana sareeramu larpinchi aayananu sevintunu
|| ento ||

Verse 2:
2. Yaatrikudanu ee jagamandu paravaasinaiti nenu
Swalpajeevitamu bhuviyandu gadipi cherudun aa desamun
Naakai vignaapanamu cheyu mahaayaajakudu yesunito
Todi vaarasudanai aayanato nityamu eledanu
|| ento ||

పల్లవి ఎంతో సుందర జీవితము - మహిమతో నిండినది

మహిమ పరచెదను - మహిమలో నుండెదను
1. దుఃఖ శ్రమలలో నన్ను రక్షించు దాగుచోటు ఆయనే
