Kadalakunduvu
Kadalakunduvu Seeyonu Kondavale
Chorus:
Kadalakunduvu seeyonu kondavale
Bedarakunduvu balamaina sinham vale (2)
Yesayya nee chenta undagaa
E chinta neekinka ledugaa (2)
Kashtamulenno kaluguchunnanu
Nitturpulenno vachiyunnanu
Dushta janamulupai dumiki tarimina
Bhrashta manushyulu nee meediki vachinaa  ||kadalakunduvu||
Neeti varadalu niluvettuna vachinaa
Nindu samudramu neellu uppongi poralinaa
Aakaasamu nundi pai agni kurasinan
Enaadu e kashtam nashtam neekundadu  ||kadalakunduvu||
Neeru kattina totavalenu
Nityam ubukuchundu neeti utavalenu
Neeti kaalvala yoranu naatabadinadai
Vardhillu vruksham vale nikshepamugaa neevunduv  ||kadalakunduvu||
