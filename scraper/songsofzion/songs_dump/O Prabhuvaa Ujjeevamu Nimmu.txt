O Prabhuvaa Ujjeevamu Nimmu
Psalms - 85:6
Chorus:
"nee prajalu nee yandu santoshinchunatlu neevu marala mammunu bratikimpavaa?" keertana psalm 856
Pallavi o prabhuvaa ujjeevamu nimmu
Virigina manas'suto ne vededanu

Verse 1:
1. Kolpoyitini modati premanu
Challaaripoye nee prema naalo
Naalo nilupumu needu prema
|| o prabhuvaa ||

Verse 2:
2. Padinavaari yaasrayamaa
Viduva badina vaari sahaayundaa
Gaayapu chetito lepumaa
|| o prabhuvaa ||

Verse 3:
3. Satruni yurilo chikkiyunnaanu
Naadu bandhakamunu tempumu neevu
Aanandamu nimmu marala naaku
|| o prabhuvaa ||

Verse 4:
4. Doshini nenu neeku sakhulaku
Aduguchuntini mannana mimmu
Kadugumu needu raktamulona
|| o prabhuvaa ||

Verse 5:
5. Aikyataye naa yabhilaasha
Naadu chedunu tolaginchumu
Satruni saktini odinchumu
|| o prabhuvaa ||

Verse 6:
6. Padina saakshyamu lepedi prabhuvaa
Vadigaa needu ujjeevamu nimmu
Budida cheyumu naaloni manshun
|| o prabhuvaa ||

Verse 7:
7. Aatma ujjeeva jvaalalu jagamuna
Kadalimpaga vyaapimpa jeyumu
Hrudayamunu phaliyimpa cheyumu
|| o prabhuvaa ||
