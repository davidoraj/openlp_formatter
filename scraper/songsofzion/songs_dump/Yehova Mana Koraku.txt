Yehova Mana Koraku
Psalm -126:3
Chorus:
"yehovaa manakoraku goppakaaryamulu chesi yunnaadu. manamu santoshabharitulamaitiviu. " keertana psalm 1263
Pallavi yehova mana koraku goppa kaaryamulanu
Jesenani manamu aanandinchedamu

Verse 1:
1. Aayana yerparachina dinamidi
Agupade manaku aascharyamugaa
Deeniyandu manamutsahinchuchu
Santoshincheda meppudu
|| yehova ||

Verse 2:
2. Tana sevakulu morapettagaane
Kanuparachenu prabhuvu ee sthalamunu
Mana mellaramunu yekamugaa kudi
Mahimanu chaatedamu
|| yehova ||

Verse 3:
3. Yaakobulo prabhu ye doshamunu
Israayelulo ye vankaratanamun
Kanugonalede prabhu yehovaa
Tanake sthuti chellintamu
|| yehova ||

Verse 4:
4. Kreeste manaku mularaayaiyunda
Pravaktalu vesiri aa punaadi
Daanimeeda kattabaditimi manamu
Tana rakshana prakatintamu
|| yehova ||

Verse 5:
5. Prati kattadamunu aayanalone
Padilamugaa namarchabadinadai
Atisundarambaina gruhamugaa
Sthutiyinchi sevintumu
|| yehova ||

Verse 6:
6. Mana prabhuvaina kreestesunandu
Parisuddha aalayamuga manamu
Nirmimpabaditimi ee bhuviyandu
Saagilapadi mrokkedamu
|| yehova ||

Verse 7:
7. Ghanadevaa neeve samastamunu
Baagugaanu chesiti vanuchu
Sthuti mahima prabhaavamulu
Neeke halleluyaa
|| yehova ||
