Prabhu Yesuke Jayamani Paadu
John - 8:32
Chorus:
"satyamu mimmunu swatantrulanugaa cheyunu" yohaanu john 832
Pallavi prabhu yesuke jayamani paadu
Paapa parihaaramunu bondi

Verse 1:
1. Yesu prabhuve maa priyundu
Vaasigaa manadu rakshakudu
Aayane mana hrudayeswarudu
Hrudaya dvaaramu teruvumu
|| prabhu ||

Verse 2:
2. Mana paapa siksha bharinchi
Tana raktamunu kaarchenu
Chakkani maargamu jupenu
Inkanu niluvaku paapamulo
|| prabhu ||

Verse 3:
3. Paapamuche bandhimpabadi
Paapamulo niluvaga nela
Yesu ninu vidipinchunu
Rakshanaartha suvaartayide
|| prabhu ||
