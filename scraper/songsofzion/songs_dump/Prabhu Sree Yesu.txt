Prabhu Sree Yesu
II Samuel - 22:29
Chorus:
"yehovaa neevu naaku deepamai yunnaavu" 2 samuyelu samuel 2229
Pallavi prabhu sree yesu
Prabhu sree yesu jayasaalee
Sarva srushti kartayu taane
Sakala jeevula paalakudu yese

Verse 1:
1. Sakala prajalaku siluvalo yesu
Swarganagariki maargamu jupi
Randi jeevajalamu traagudanuchu
Nindaina premato pilchuchunnaadu
|| prabhu ||

Verse 2:
2. Jeevana jyotiyai veluguchunnaadu
Premato mammula veliginchinaadu
Paapaandhakaaramunu baapinaadu
Paavanaparachenu mammulanu
|| prabhu ||

Verse 3:
3. Paapulu paschaattaapamu nondi
Paapula praapakudesuni jeri
Paapavimuktini bondi meeru
Paavanu desuni sankeertinchudi
|| prabhu ||
