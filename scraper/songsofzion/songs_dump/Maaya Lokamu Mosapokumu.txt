Maaya Lokamu Mosapokumu
Eccl - 1:2
Chorus:
"vyarthamu vyarthamu samastamu vyarthame" prasangi echlesiastes 12
Pallavi maaya lokamu mosapokumu
Yesunande rakshana dorukunu neeku

Verse 1:
1. Bandhumitrulu bhaarya biddalu
Dhanamu ghanamu andachandamu
Avi anniyunu neetibudagale
Samaadhitone samaaptamaunu
|| maaya ||

Verse 2:
2. Padaga vippina paamuvale nee
Paapamu ninnu vembadinchunu
Paapa phalitamu maranamegadaa
Daani antamu nityanarakamu
|| maaya ||

Verse 3:
3. Ninnu rakshimpanenchi prabhuvu
Paramunu vidachi dharakuvachenu
Neeku badulugaa sramala norchenu
Siluvalone baliyaaye nesu
|| maaya ||

Verse 4:
4. Ippudainanu oppukonumika
Nee paapajeevitamu nantayu
Ninnu kshaminchunu krupato prabhuvu
Idiye nee rakshana dinamugaa
|| maaya ||

Verse 5:
5. Dehamanta prabhu gaayamul ponde
Parisuddha raktamunu
Kaarchenu nityajeevamu neeku neeyanu
Mrutini gelchi tirigilechenu
|| maaya ||
