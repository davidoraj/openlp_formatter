Jagati Karudenche Rakshanakarta
I Timothy -1:15
Chorus:
"paapulanu rakshinchutaku kreestu yesu lokamunaku vachenu" 1 timoti timothy 115
Pallavi jagati karudenche rakshanakarta
Paapula mora vinenu yesu

Verse 1:
1. Krunginavaarini ningiki yetti paapamu saapamu
Aapadalanni baapenu yesu
|| jagati ||

Verse 2:
2. Paapapupaatranu paanamu jesi
Maranapu mullunu virachi jayinchi
Terache mokshadvaaramun
|| jagati ||

Verse 3:
3. Lendi randi paaduchu vega
Chaateda melleda kotla prajalaku
Premaamayu desun
|| jagati ||
