Devudoka Nagaramu Mana Korakai
Heb.11:10
Chorus:
"elayanagaa devudu deniki silpiyu nirmaanakudunai yunnaado, punaadulugala aa pattanamukoraku. . . eduruchuchuchundenu. " hebree hebrews 1110
Pallavi devudoka nagaramu mana korakai
Siddhaparachuchunde nundutakai
Manamu velledam nischayamugaa

Verse 1:
1. Melimi bangaaramu adbhuta nagaram
Viluvaina raallato punaadi vesenu
Raktamuche suddhulai kshaminchabaditimi
Manamu velledam nischayamugaa nischayamugaa
|| devudoka ||

Verse 2:
2. Nagara praakaaramulu ettainavi
Surya kaantamulache kattabadenu
Prabhuve vimochinchi adhikaaramichenu
Manamu velledam nischayamugaa nischayamugaa
|| devudoka ||

Verse 3:
3. Mutyaala gummamulu pandrendu galavu
Panniddaru dutalu achata nundiri
Parisuddha jeevitam nerpinchenu prabhuvu
Manamu velledam nischayamugaa nischayamugaa
|| devudoka ||

Verse 4:
4. Aa nagara veedhulu melimi bangaaram
Dvaaramulu eppatiki muyabadavu
Aarbhaatanto pravesinchi vijayotsavambuto
Manamu velledam nischayamugaa nischayamugaa
|| devudoka ||

Verse 5:
5. Gorrepilla sinhaasana machatundunu
Aayane deepamu naalayamai yundunu
Jeevagranthamanduna perulunnavanuchu
Manamu velledam nischayamugaa nischayamugaa
|| devudoka ||

Verse 6:
6. Jayavantula korake aa nagaram
Paapaatmulu achataku vellaneraru
Vijayula maudamu kreestuni raktamuche
Manamu velledam nischayamugaa nischayamugaa
|| devudoka ||

పరిశుద్ధ జీవితం నేర్పించెను ప్రభువు

4. ఆ నగర వీధులు మేలిమి బంగారం
