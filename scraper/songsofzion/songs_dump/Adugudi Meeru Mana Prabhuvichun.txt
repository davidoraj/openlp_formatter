Adugudi Meeru Mana Prabhuvichun
Mathew - 7:7
Chorus:
"adugudi mee kiyyabadunu" mattayi matthew 77
Pallavi adugudi meeru mana prabhuvichun
Tappaka yichun
Adigedi prativaadu pondun

Verse 1:
1. Bhaktulu viswaasamuto praarthinchi pondiri
Vaari jeevitamula nenchi chuchi
Mee melu korakai praarthinchi pondu
|| adugudi ||

Verse 2:
2. Manalo tana saktikoladi anugrahinchunu
Uhakuminchi cheyu devunini
Mee melu korakai praarthinchi pondu
|| adugudi ||

Verse 3:
3. Mana praarthanala naalakinchi santrupti chendunu
Tana naamamunaku mahima kaluga
Mee melu korakai praarthinchi pondu
|| adugudi ||

Verse 4:
4. Idivaraku meeremiyu adugakuntiri
Meeraananda bharitulagunatlu
Mee melu korakai praarthinchi pondu
|| adugudi ||

Verse 5:
5. Devuni kedainanu cheya nasaadhyamaa
Sarvasaktigala mana devunadigi
Prabhu mahima korakai praarthinchi pondu
|| adugudi ||
