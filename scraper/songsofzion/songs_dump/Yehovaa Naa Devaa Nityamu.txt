Yehovaa Naa Devaa Nityamu
Psalm - 27:1
Chorus:
"yehovaa bhaktulaaraa, aayananu keertinchudi. aayana parisuddhamaina gnaapakaartha naamamunu batti aayananu sthutinchudi. " keertana psalm 30
Pallavi yehovaa naa devaa nityamu
Nenu ninnu sthutiyincheda
Halleluya halleluya
Halleluya halleluya

Verse 1:
1. Yehovaa naa satruvulanu
Naa pai santoshimpa neeyaka
Neevu nannuddharinchinandukai nenu
Ninnu koniyaaduchunnaanu
|| yehovaa ||

Verse 2:
2. Nenu neeku morapettagaa
Neevu nan swasthaparachitivi
Parisuddha gnaapakaartha naamamunu batti
Bhaktulaaraa prabhun keertinchudu
|| yehovaa ||

Verse 3:
3. Yehovaa paataalamulo nundi
Naa praanamu levadeesitivi
Nenu samaadhiloki digakundaga
Neevu nannu bratikinchitivi
|| yehovaa ||

Verse 4:
4. Aayana kopamu nimishame
Daya aayushkaalamundunu
Edpu vachi raatriyundina udaya
Muna santoshamu kalugunu
|| yehovaa ||

Verse 5:
5. Ne nennadu kadalanani naa
Kshema kaalamuna talachiti
Yehovaa daya kaligi neeve naa
Parvatamu sthiraparachitivi
|| yehovaa ||

Verse 6:
6. Nee mukhamu neevu daachina
Yapudu nenu kshobhilliti
Yehovaa neeke mora pettitini
Naa prabhuvunu bratimaalu kontini
|| yehovaa ||

Verse 7:
7. Nenu samaadhiloki digina
Naa praanamu valana laabhamaa
Mannu ninnu sthutinchunaa?
Nee satyamunu gurchi adi vivarinchunaa?
|| yehovaa ||

Verse 8:
8. Naa angalaarpunu neevegaa
Naatyamugaa maarchiyuntivi
Naa gonepatta vidipinchi santosha
Vastramu dharimpa jesitivi
|| yehovaa ||
