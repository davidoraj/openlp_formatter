Yesu Manatonundaga
Acts - 4:13
Chorus:
"vaaru yesuto kuda undina vaarani gurterigiri" apostalula kaaryamulu achts 413
Pallavi yesu manatonundaga dhairyamugaa saaguchu
Kathina maargamainanu venukaku tirugamu

Verse 1:
1. Paata sangatulanniyu gatinche marala raavuga
Yesunandu krottavai nutanamuga nadupunu
Yesu prabhuni yaagnalu adbhutamuga dorikenu
Parama darsanamandundi tolagipomu yennadu
|| yesu ||

Verse 2:
2. Saitaanu sareeramu lokamuto poraaduchu
Yenta krayamunainanu santoshamuga chellintumu
Yuddha mandu jayamonda prabhuni sakti pondedam
Yesu jayamu ponduchu saataanu raajyamanachedam
|| yesu ||

Verse 3:
3. Duḥkha roga baadhalu kaligina chintinchaka
Yesu naamamanduna pondedamu saktini
Alpakaala yaatralo gonugu sanugu lundaka
Prati paristhitiyandu truptichendi yundumu
|| yesu ||

Verse 4:
4. Kreestu yesu manasune manamu kaligiyundamu
Eershya krodha virodhamul vidachi prema chupedam
Naagatimeeda chetini vunchi venuka tirugamu
Yesu vaipu chuchuchu aayanatoda nadachedam
|| yesu ||

Verse 5:
5. Manalo paata purushuni champedamu siluvalo
Antaranga purushunandu balamunu pondedamu
Aatmeeya jeevitambulo unnata sthaanamu pondedam
Sareera yichhalanniyu anagadrokki vesedam
|| yesu ||

Verse 6:
6. Vidachedam eelokamu yaatra purti chesedam
Parama raajyamanduna parama gruhamu cheredam
Yesu prabhunu sandhinchedam aanandamuto nindedam
Bahumaanamulu pondedam sadaa aayanato nundedam
|| yesu ||
