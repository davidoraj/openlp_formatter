---
Yesuni Nindanu Bharinchi
Heb. 13:13
Chorus:
"kaabatti manamaayana nindanu bharinchuchu sibiramu velupaliki aayana yoddaku velludamu" hebree hebrews 1313
Pallavi yesuni nindanu bharinchi
Aayana yoddaku velludamu

Verse 1:
1. Sanballatu tobeeyaa cheyu helanalu
Vaaralu pettu sramala bharinchi
Aayanato santoshamuga bharinchi
Bayaluderi velludamu
|| yesuni ||

Verse 2:
2. Stephanu chaavunu madilo dalachi
Peturu cheralo nunduta dalachi
Mosamu lenniyo kalginagaani
Bayaluderi velludamu
|| yesuni ||

Verse 3:
3. Kapata sodarula valana baadhalu
Apanindalu maa kenniyunna
Yesu prabhuvune dveshinchinanu
Bayaluderi velludamu
|| yesuni ||

Verse 4:
4. Karralatone mamu kottinanu
Kattulato mamu khandinchinanu
Prajala valana bahugaa naliginanu
Bayalu deri velludamu
|| yesuni ||

Verse 5:
5. Agnilo naduva valasina gaani
Paluvidha sramalu maa pai badinan
Champabadina gorrepillanu juchi
Bayaluderi velludamu
|| yesuni ||

Verse 6:
6. Nibbaramuga niluchundumu memu
Yehovaaye maa pakshamu jeri
Poraatamulo jayamu chekurchun
Halleluya paadudamu
|| yesuni ||
