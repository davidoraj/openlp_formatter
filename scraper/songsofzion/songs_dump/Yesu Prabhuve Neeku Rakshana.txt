Yesu Prabhuve Neeku Rakshana
Heb - 2:4
Chorus:
"inta goppa rakshananu manamu nirlakshyamuchesinayedala elaagu tappinchukondumu?" hebree hebrews 23
Pallavi yesu prabhuve neeku rakshana nichunu
Inta goppa rakshana nirlakshya pettaku

Verse 1:
1. Mrutiye paapapu jeevamu prabhuni varamu jeevamu
Kreestu nangeekarinchina nityajeeva michunu
Praanamun aa siluvapai ni baligaa nidi nin konegadaa
Maratuvaa yesuni premanu maruvaku
|| yesu prabhuve ||

Verse 2:
2. Kantiki kanabadani venno cheviki vanabadanivenno
Grahimpa sakyamu kaanivi neekai siddapadenu
Praanamun aa siluvapaini baligaa nidi nin konegadaa
Pedachevin pettaku yesuni maatanu
|| yesu prabhuve ||

Verse 3:
3. Marana balamu galavaanin naasanambu chesen
Marana bhayamulo nunna vaarini vidipinchenu
Praanamun aa siluvapaini baligaa nidi nin konegadaa
Hrudayamun teruvumu yesunu cherchuko
|| yesu prabhuve ||

Verse 4:
4. Siluva raktamu chetanu nirdevulaina vaarini
Madhyapu godanu padagotti aikyaparache devunito
Praanamun aa siluvapaini baligaa nidi nin konegadaa
Samaadhaanapadu mika devunito yeekshaname
|| yesu prabhuve ||

Verse 5:
5. Daivaswabhaavambulo parisuddhaatmuni yandu
Paralokapu pilupulo paalivaarala jesenu
Praanamun aa siluvapaini baligaa nidi nin konegadaa
Parisuddhula swaasthyamulo paalibhaaga mondumu
|| yesu prabhuve ||

Verse 6:
6. Devuni parisuddhatalo kalugabovu mahimalo
Kreestuyesu prabhuvulo paalivaarala jesenu
Praanamun aa siluvapaini baligaa nidi nin konegadaa
Tvarapadu needu bhaagamunu pondanu
|| yesu prabhuve ||

Verse 7:
7. Paralokamulo kanabadu tellanaina vastramul
Dharinchukonu samuhamu kharjura mattalu chebhuni
Devunikini gorrepillakunu stotramulu cheyuchunda
Neevu nee rakshanakai paaduvu halleluya
|| yesu prabhuve ||

పరిశుద్ధుల స్వాస్థ్యములో పాలిభాగ - మొందుము
|| యేసు ప్రభువే ||

త్వరపడు నీదు భాగమును పొందను
