Manamu Yesu Prabhuni Mahima
John -1:14
Chorus:
"tandrivalana kaligina adviteeya kumaaruni mahimavale manamu aayana mahimanu kanugontimi" yohaanu john 114
Pallavi manamu yesu prabhuni mahima kanugontimi

Verse 1:
1. Tandrinundi kaligina ekaika putruni mahima
Vale aayana mahimanu kanugontimi
|| manamu ||

Verse 2:
2. Neevu nammina yedala devuni mahima chutuvu
Jeeva punarut'thaanamu yesu
|| manamu ||

Verse 3:
3. Pratyakshagudaaramunu meghamu kammagaane
Yehovaa tejas'su nindenu
|| manamu ||

Verse 4:
4. Devuni nivaasamu manujulato nunnadi
Nivasinchu devude vaarilo
|| manamu ||

Verse 5:
5. Mahimaa prabhaavamuto makutamu dharinchina kreestu
Tanayulanu teche mahimaku
|| manamu ||

Verse 6:
6. Mana rakshana kartanu sramato sampurnuni jeya
Mana tandrike tagiyunnadi
|| manamu ||

Verse 7:
7. Mana chulakani sramalu raabovu mahima yeduta
Ennataginavi kaavu
|| manamu ||

Verse 8:
8. Parisuddha pattanamulo devunike mahima
Prabhuvunake mahima halleluya
|| manamu ||
