Nee Vaatma Yuddhamulo Jaya
II Timothy - 2:3
Chorus:
"kreestuyokka manchi raanuva vaanivale naatokuda sramanu anubhavinchumu" 2 timoti timothy 23
Pallavi nee vaatma yuddhamulo jaya mondumu
O naa sodaraa venukanja veyaku
O naa sodari venukanja veyaku

Verse 1:
1. Swaami yesu tana siluvanu mosi
Saitaanu raajyamu purtigaa jayinche
Neevunu yesuni vembadi vellumaa
O naa sodaraa venukanja veyaku
O naa sodari venukanja veyaku
|| nee vaatma ||

Verse 2:
2. Siramuna mundla makutamu dharinchi
Sipaayiche talapai kottabade
Nee siras'su natle samarpinchu
O naa sodaraa venukanja veyaku
O naa sodari venukanja veyaku
|| nee vaatma ||

Verse 3:
3. Kalvari paiki ontarigaane
Vellenu yesu tyaaga sahitudai
Neevunu aa daarine vellumaa
O naa sodaraa venukanja veyaku
O naa sodari venukanja veyaku
|| nee vaatma ||

Verse 4:
4. Lokastulantaa nidrinchuchundagaa
Mokaallapai yesu kondapai nunde
Neevunu praarthana sakti nondumaa
O naa sodaraa venukanja veyaku
O naa sodari venukanja veyaku
|| nee vaatma ||

Verse 5:
5. Saahasamuna nee vaatma khadgamuna
Saitaanu bandhamu khandinchivesi
Kreestuni sanghamaa mundanja veyumaa
O naa sodaraa venukanja veyaku
O naa sodari venukanja veyaku
|| nee vaatma ||
