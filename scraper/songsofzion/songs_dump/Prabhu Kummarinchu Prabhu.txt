Prabhu Kummarinchu Prabhu
Isiah - 44:3
Chorus:
"nenu dappigalavaanimeeda neellanu yendina bhumimeeda pravaaha jalamulanu kummarinchedanu" yeshayaa isaiah 443
Pallavi prabhu kummarinchu prabhu kummarinchu
Deevenala varshamu kummarinchumu

Verse 1:
1. Omaa yesu vinu maamanavi
Maa madhyaku prabhu raarammu
Nee dvaaramuna cheri yunnaamu
Nimpumu prabhuvaa mammulanu
|| prabhu ||

Verse 2:
2. Dappigonna vaariki jalamu
Yendina bhumiki jaladhaaral
Nee santatipai aatmanu pampu
Nee deevenalato nimpumu
|| prabhu ||

Verse 3:
3. Tandri kudiprakkana niluchunna
Yesu prabhuvunu chupumu
Aa mahimaa nivaasamuloni
Samruddhito mammu nimpumu
|| prabhu ||

Verse 4:
4. Rendu kerubula madhya nundi
Madhura swaramu vinanimmu
Yesuni tappa yevarini vinaka
Aatmalo trupti pondedamu
|| prabhu ||

Verse 5:
5. Teruvabadina paramunuchuda
Dutalu yekkuchu diguchunda
Nee sanghamunu abhishekinchi
Ghanakaaryamulanu jariginchu
|| prabhu ||

|| ప్రభు ||

ఆ మహిమా నివాసములోని
