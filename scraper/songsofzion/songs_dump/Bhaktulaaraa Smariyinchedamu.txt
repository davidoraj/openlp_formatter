Bhaktulaaraa Smariyinchedamu
Turn yad karo sab bhakto
Chorus:
"eeyana samastamunu baagugaa chesiyunnaadu. " maarku mark 737
Pallavi bhaktulaaraa smariyinchedamu
Prabhuchesina melulannitini
Adigi uhinchu vaatikanna
Mari sarvamu chakkaga jese

Verse 1:
1. Sreeyese mana siras'sai yundi
Mahaabalasurundu
Sarvamu nichenu tana hastamuto
Ento dayagala vaadu
|| bhaktulaaraa ||

Verse 2:
2. Gaali tuphaanulanu gaddinchi
Baadhalanu tolaginche
Sramalalo manaku todaiyundi
Bayalu parache tana jayamun
|| bhaktulaaraa ||

Verse 3:
3. Jeeva nadini pravahimpajese
Sakala sthalambula yandu
Lekkakuminchina aatmalateche
Pabhuve stotraar'hundu
|| bhaktulaaraa ||

Verse 4:
4. Apostalula, pravaktalanu
Suvaartikulanu yiche
Sanghamu abhivruddhini chendutaku
Sevakulandariniche
|| bhaktulaaraa ||

Verse 5:
5. Mana pakshamuna taane poraadi
Saitaanunu odinche
Intavarakunu aadukonenugaa
Tana mahaatmyamu jupe
|| bhaktulaaraa ||

Verse 6:
6. Ee bhuviyandu jeevinchukaalamu
Bratikedamu prabhukorake
Manamaayana karpinchukonedamu
Aayana aasayamadiye
|| bhaktulaaraa ||

Verse 7:
7. Konchemu kaalame migili yunnadi
Prabhuvunu sandhinchutakai
Ganuka manamu nadachukonedamu
Prabhu maargamula yandu
|| bhaktulaaraa ||

శ్రమలలో మనకు తోడైయుండి

3. జీవ నదిని ప్రవహింపజేసె

సంఘము అభివృద్ధిని చెందుటకు
సేవకులందరినిచ్చె
|| భక్తులారా ||
5. మన పక్షమున తానే పోరాడి
