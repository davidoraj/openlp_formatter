Chudumu Ee Kshaname Kalvarini
Luke - 23:33
Chorus:
"vaaru kalvari anabadina sthalamunaku vachinappudu" lukaa luke 2333
Pallavi chudumu ee kshaname kalvarini
Premaa prabhuvu neekai niluchundenu
Goppa rakshananivva sree yesudu
Siluvalo vrelaadu chunnaadugaa

Verse 1:
1. Maanavu lento chedipoyiri
Maraninchedamani talapoyaka
Erugaru maranamu nikkamani
Narakamunnadani vaarerugaru
|| chudumu ||

Verse 2:
2. Ihamandu neeku kalavanniyu
Chanipovu samayaana ventaraavu
Chanipoyinanu neevu lechedavu
Teerpunnadani erugu oka dinamuna
|| chudumu ||

Verse 3:
3. Manalanu dhanavantulugaa cheyanu
Daridrudaayenu mana prabhuvu
Raktamu kaarchenu paapulakai
Angeekarinchumu sree yesuni
|| chudumu ||

Verse 4:
4. Siluvapai chudumu aa priyuni
Aa premakai neevu yemittuvu
Arpinchuko needu jeevitamu
Aayana korakai jeevinchumu
|| chudumu ||
