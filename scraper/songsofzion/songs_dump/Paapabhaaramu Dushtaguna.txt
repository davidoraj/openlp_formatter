Paapabhaaramu Dushtaguna
Gal.-1:4
Chorus:
"aayana manalanu prastutapu dushta kaalamulo nundi vimochimpa valenani mana paapamula nimittamu tannu taane appaginchukonenu" galatee galatians 14
Paapabhaaramu dushtaguna sametundanai
Duḥkhanashta daurjanyamuto nunda
Chinna swaramu vinabade nannu piluchunatlugaa
Rammu pondu naadusilva visraanti
Pallavi silvalo silvalo paapamella boye silvayanduna
Bhaaramella veedenu raatri pagalaayenu
Kanugonti silvalo rakshana

Verse 1:
1. Eguchundu nityamu jeevamunu bondanu
Silvalo krupasakti pondudun
Sodhanalu vachina jaya monduchundunu
Mokarinchi praarthintu silvachenta
|| silvalo ||

Verse 2:
2. Jeevitamandu edpu baadha sodhanlalella
Aatmasaayamuche silvajutunu
Yesu paapikorakupaddapaatlu chudagaa
Bhaaramula moyusakti pondudun
|| silvalo ||

Verse 3:
3. Bhaaramuche krungina paapee nede raarammu
Yesu raktamandu ninnu kadugunu
Nityajeeva michunu ninnu samarpinchuko
Kreestu yesu krotta sakti nichunu
|| silvalo ||
