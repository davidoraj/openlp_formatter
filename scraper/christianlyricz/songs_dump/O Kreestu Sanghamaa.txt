Chorus:
O kreestu sanghamaa, parisuddha sanghamaa
Prabhuvu naamamulo saage anubandhamaa
O priya sanghamaa, yesayya dehamaa
O kanta kanneeru tagadu sahavaasamaa
Prati kashtamu manamu panchukundaamu
Kalisi andaramu vedukundaamu
(2) ||o kreestu||
Manamantaa kalisi aa devuni dehamu
Tandri chittamugaa erpadina sanghamu
(2)
E bhaagamu srama padinaa kaligenu vedana
Okkariki ghanatayinaa andariki aadarana
(2) ||prati kashtamu||
Saati sodarulu sramala paalainapudu
Saataanu saktulache sodhimpabadinapudu
(2)
Dhairyamunu nimpaali, viswaasamu penchaali
Edategaka praarthinchi sodhananu gelavaali
(2) ||prati kashtamu||
Sramalu pondevaaru avidheyulu kaaru
Viswaasamu penchukoni devunilo edigeru
(2)

(2)  ||ఓ క్రీస్తు||
