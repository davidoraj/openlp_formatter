Chorus:
Kallallo kanneerenduku gundello digulenduku
Ika neevu kalata chendaku
Nemmadi lekunnadaa gundello gaayamainadaa
Ika avi undabovugaa
Yese nee rakshana yese nee nireekshana
(2) ||kallallo||
Horu gaalulu veechagaa tuphaanulu chelaregagaa
Maata maatram selaveeyagaa nimmalamaayenugaa
(2)
Yese nee naavikaa bhayamu chendaku neevika
Yese nee rakshakaa kalata chendaku neevika
||kallallo||
Karuvu khadgamulochinaa ninda vedana chuttinaa
Lokamantaa ekamainaa bhayamu chendakumaa
(2)
Yese nee rakshakaa digulu chendaku neevika
Yese vimochakaa santasinchumu neevika
||kallallo||
