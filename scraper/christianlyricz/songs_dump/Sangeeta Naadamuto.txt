Chorus:
Sangeeta naadamuto stotra sankeertanato
Nee prema geetam paadeda
Nee goppa kaaryam chaateda
Naa jeevitam maarchina yesayyaa
Ee nee runam teerchuta etulayyaa
||sangeeta||
Naa kathina hrudayamuna kaarunyamunu nimpi
Kaluvalu puyinchina krupalanu koniyaadeda
(2)
Paapamulu kshamiyinchi nanu maarchina
Doshamulu bhariyinchi daricherchina
||nee prema||
Naa kashta samayamuna naa chentane nilachi
Viduvaka nadipinchina vidhamunu vivarincheda
(2)
Kshemamunu kaliginchi nanu lepina
Deevenalu kuripinchi krupachupina
||nee prema||
Naa duḥkha dinamulalo odaarpu kaliginchi
Kanneetini tudichina kramamunu prakatincheda
(2)
Vaakyamuto darsinchi balaparichina
Satyamuto sandhinchi sthiraparichina
||nee prema||
