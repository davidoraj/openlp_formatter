Chorus:
Aakaasa pakshulanu chudandi
Avi vittavu avi koyavu
Gariselalo daachukovu
Kotlalo kurchukovu
||aakaasa||
Anudinamu kaavalasina aahaaramu
Andajeyunu vaatiki aa devudu
Kalasikattugaa avi egiri potaayi
Kadupu nimpukonipoyi marala tirigi vastaayi
||aakaasa||
Swaardhamu vanchana vaatikundadu
Saativaani dochukone manasu undadu
Repatini gurchina chinta undadu
Puta elaa gadapaalani baadha undadu
||aakaasa||
Pakshulanu poshinche aa devudu
Manushyulanu poshinchuta maaniveyunaa
Srushtilona manishi bratuku sreshtamu kadaa
Prabhuvu todu undagaa manaku enduku baadha
||aakaasa||

కలసికట్టుగా అవి ఎగిరి పోతాయి
