Sthuti paadanaa nenu
Nannu kaache yesayyaa
Naa jeevaannadaataku
Nanu nadipe prabhuvuku
Paapamulo padiyunna vela
Vadalakane dari cherchina daata
Nee divya kaantilo nadipinchumu yesayyaa
||sthuti||
Solipoyi tulutunna vela
Jaalito nanu pilachina naa devaa
Naa hrudaya dhyaanamu neeke arpintunu
||sthuti||
Bhuminele raaraaju neevani
Dharaniloni nee mahimanu prakatincha
Nee rekkala chaatuna nanu daache needavani
||sthuti||
