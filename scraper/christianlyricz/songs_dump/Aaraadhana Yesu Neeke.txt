Aaraadhana yesu neeke
(4)
Nee chittam nenu jaripeda
Chupinche maarganlo nadicheda
Nee sannidhilo ne nilicheda
Naa priya yesuve
(2) ||aaraadhana||
Samudram meeda nadache mee adbhuta paadamul
Maa munde meeru unnappudu ledu bhayamu
Gaali samudramu lobade mee adbhuta maatalaku
Mee maddatu maaku unappudu ledu kalavaram
(2) ||aaraadhana||
Daari anta andhakaaranlo chutti unnappudu
Daari chupe yesu unte naaku ledu kalavaram
Pharo sainyam vembadinchi nannu chutti unnappudu
Rakshinche yehova unte ledu bhayamu
(2) ||aaraadhana||
