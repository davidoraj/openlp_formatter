Chorus:
Sannutintu yesu swaami ninnu anudinam
Nee mahaatya kaaryamulanu paadi vivarintunu
(2)
Sodhana vedana kashta samayaana naa todugaa nunduvu
Aascharya kaaryamulu aananda ghadiyalu ennadu maruvanu
||sannutintu||
Samaadhilonundi naa praanamu vimochinchiyunnaavu
Karunaa kataakshamulu kireetamugaa naakichiyunnaavu
(2)
Naa doshamulannitini kshamiyinchinaavu karuna samruddhudavu
Melulato naa hrudayam truptiparachaavu neekemi chellintunu
||sannutintunu||
Mahimaiswaryamula mahaaraaju mahimato nimpunu
Saanti raajya sthaapakudu tana saanti nichunu
(2)
Adigina vaariki kaadanakunda varamulu kuripinchunu
Yesayya nee goppa naamamu smariyimpa naakento bhaagyamu
||sannutintunu||
