Yesu raktame jayamu jayamuraa

(2) ||yesu||
Balaheenulaku balamaina durgamu mukti yesu raktamu
Vyaadhi baadhalaku vidudala kaliginchunu swasthata yesu raktamu
(2)
Saantiki sthaavaram sree yesuni raktam
Neetiki kavacham parisuddhuni raktam
(2)
Mrutyuvune geluchu raktamu
Paataalam muyu raktamu
Narakaanni bandhinchina
Jayaseeli adhipati raaraaju yesayye
||yesu||
Paapiki saranam yesu raktamu rakshana praakaaramu
Apavitraatmanu paaradrolunu khadgamu yesu raktamu
(2)
Satruvu niluvadu virodhi evvadu?
E aayudhamu neepai vardhilladu
(2)
Saataanne nalaggottina
Vaadi talane chitaggottina
Kodama sinhamai meghaarudhigaa
Teerpu teerchavachu raaraaju yesayye
