Chorus:
Maa goppa devaa mamu karuninchi
Atyunnata sthaanamulo nanu nilipaavu
Yogyudane kaanu aa premaku
Vela kattalenu aa premaku
Aaraadhinchedanu… naa purna hrudayamuto
Ninne keertintunu naa jeevitamantaa
(2)
Nemmade leni bratukulo paapapu bandhakaalalo
Chikki unna nannu neevu vidipinchaavu
(2)
Paapanlo nundi nanu vimochinchutaku
Aa ghora siluvalona maraninchaavu
Daasyamulonundi padi unna nannu
Nee kumaarunigaa rakshinchaavu
||maa goppa||
Maarpuleni bratukulo malinamaina manas'suto
Ne tulanaadi dushinchindi ninnenegaa
(2)
Aa sthitilo kudaa nanu preminche goppa
Hrudayam neede yesayyaa
Naalaanti ghoramaina paapini kudaa
Kshamiyinchi preminchindi neevenayyaa
||maa goppa||
