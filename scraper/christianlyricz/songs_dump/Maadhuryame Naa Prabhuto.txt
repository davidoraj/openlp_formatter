Chorus:
Maadhuryame naa prabhuto jeevitam
Mahimaanandame mahaa aascharyame
||maadhuryame||
Sarva sareerulu gaddini polina vaaraiyunnaaru
Vaari andamantayu puvvu vale
Vaadipovunu vaadipovunu
||maadhuryame||
Nemmadi lekundaa vistaaramaina dhanamunduta kante
Devuni yandali bhaya bhaktulato
Undute melu undute melu
||maadhuryame||
Naa vimochana kraya dhanamunu chellinchenu prabhuve
Naa rogamantayu siluvalo
Pariharinchenu pariharinchenu
||maadhuryame||
Vaadabaarani kireetamunakai nannu pilichenu
Tejovaasulaina parisuddhulato
Epudu cheredano epudu cheredano
||maadhuryame||
