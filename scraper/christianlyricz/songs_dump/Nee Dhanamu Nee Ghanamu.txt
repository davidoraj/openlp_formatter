Chorus:
Nee dhanamu nee ghanamu prabhu yesude
Nee dasamaa bhaagamuneeya venudeetuvaa venudeetuvaa
||nee dhanamu||
Dharalona dhana dhaanyamula neeyagaa
Karuninchi kaapaadi rakshimpagaa
(2)
Paraloka naadhundu neekeeyagaa
Mari yesu korakeeya venudeetuvaa
||nee dhanamu||
Paadipantalu prabhuvu neekeeyagaa
Kudu guddalu neeku dayacheyagaa
(2)
Vedanga prabhu yesu naamambunu
Gaduvela prabhukeeya no kraistavaa
||nee dhanamu||
Velugu needalu gaali varshambulu
Kaliginche prabhu neeku uchitambugaa
(2)
Veligincha dhara paini prabhu naamamu
Kalimi koladi prabhuna karpimpavaa
||nee dhanamu||
Kaliginche sakalambu samruddigaa
Tolaginche palubhaadha bharitambulu
(2)
Baliyaaye nee paapamula kesuve
Cheluvanga prabhukeeya chintintuvaa
||nee dhanamu||
