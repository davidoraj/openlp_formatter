Krotta yedu modalu bettenu mana bratuku nandu

Tattara padakunda jeyu tuttamottamambu juda
||krotta||
Pondiyunna melulanniyu bonkambu meera
Dendamandu smarana jeyudi
Indu meeru modalupettu pandemandu gelva valayu
Andamuganu ravini boli alayakundaa melayakundaa
||krotta||
Balamu leni vaaramayyeenu balamondavachu
Kalimi meera garta vaakkuna
Alayakundaa aduguchunda nalagakundaa modamondi
Balamosangu sarva vidhula nelami meera nochuchunda
||krotta||
Paapa pankamantinappudu prabhu kreestu yesu
Praapu jeri meeru vedagaa
Sepu meera tanadu karuna paapamantaa kadigivesi
Paapa roga chihnalanni baapi vesi suddi cheyu
||krotta||

డెందమందు స్మరణ జేయుడి

||క్రొత్త||

||క్రొత్త||
