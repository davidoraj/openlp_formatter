(2)
Stutulu mahima ghanata neeke
(2)
||devaa nee||
Saaswata premato nanu preminchi
Paramunu veedi bhuvikarudenchi
Kaluvari siluvalo raktamu kaarchi
Needu krupato nanu rakshinchina
Nee divya premanu atyadhikamugaa
Smarintun jeevita kaalamantaa
||devaa nee||
Nee kantipaapagaa nannu kaachi
Nee cheti needalo nannu daachi
Nee arachetilo nanu chekkukoni
Needu sottugaa nanu chesukoni
Akshayamaina nee madhura premanu
Deekshato ilalo chaatedanu
||devaa nee||
