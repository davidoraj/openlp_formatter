Priya Yesu Dehamulo
https://christianlyricz.com/2021/04/21/priya-yesu-dehamulo/
Chorus:
Priya yesu dehamulo ubike raktapu uta
Pravahinche erulai kalushambulanu kadugaa
Koradaala debbalache dehamu chaaralai cheelagaa
Sundarundu vikaarudaaye chudanollani vaadaaye
||priya yesu||
Naa dushta talampulakai mundla kireetamaa talapai
Naa neecha nadatalakai paada hastamulalo cheelalaa
||priya yesu||
Mukhamupai gruddinanu chempalapai kottinanu
Ballemu prakkalo dimpinaa neechuniki nitya jeevamaa
||priya yesu||
Idi entati prema prabhu emani varnintu ninnu
Sajeeva yaagamugaa nanne neekarpintunu

  ||ప్రియ యేసు||

  ||ప్రియ యేసు||
