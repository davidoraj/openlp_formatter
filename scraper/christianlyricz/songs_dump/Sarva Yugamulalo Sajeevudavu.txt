(2) ||sarva yugamulalo||

Srunga dhvanulato sainyamu nadipinchuvaadavu neeve
(2)

Maranamu gelichina bahu dheerudaa
(2) ||sarva yugamulalo||

Bahu taramulaku sobhaatisayamugaa chesitivi nannu
