(2)
Needu swaramu vinakane nenu
Ninu vidachi tirigiti nenu
Naadu bratukulo samastamu kolipoyiti
(2) ||ninu||
Nee divya premanu vidachi nee aatma todu trosivesi
Andhakaara trovalo nadachi nee gaayame repitini
(2)
Ayinaa ade prema nanu cherchukunnaprema
Nanu veedani karuna maruvalenayyaa yesayyaa
||neeto||
Nanu hattukunna prema nanu cherchukunna prema
Nee velugulone nityam ne nadichedan
(2)
Nanu viduvaku priyudaa naaku todugaa naduvu
Neetone naa bratuku saagintunu yesayyaa
||ninu||
