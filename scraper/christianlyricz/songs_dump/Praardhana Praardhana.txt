Praardhana praardhana
Prabhunito sambhaashana
Praardhane upiri
Praardhane kaapari
||praardhana||
Kanneeti upavaasa praardhana
Saataanu saktulapai vijayamu
(2)
Virigi naligina vignaapana praardhana
Jayamu nosagunu jeevitamulu
||praardhana||
Oleeva kondala praardhana
Swasthata nosagunu vyaadhi baadhalaku
(2)
Prabhuvu nerpina getsemane praardhana
Aatmala nosagunu sevalo
||praardhana||
Siluvalo nerpina praardhana
Premanu nerpunu bratukuna
(2)
Saataanu choranu chotu lenidi
Paapamunu dari raaneeyanidi
||praardhana||
