#Prayer Life of a Believer
==
#Understanding of a prayer:
P - Perfect alignment with God’s heart and His will
R - Revere God
A - Adoration/abide with Christ’s heart and His will
Y - yielding to God’s heart and His will
E - Earnest payer
R - Ready to accept the answer
==
#Individual prayer
- Individual prayer refer to the act of communicating with a higher power
- It is a time for personal reflection, confession, thanksgiving, and petition, allowing for an intimate relationship with the Creator.
- It is a living relationship
==

Matthew 6:6
But you, when you pray, go into your room, and when you have shut your door, pray to your Father who is in the secret place; and your Father who sees in secret will reward you openly

#Intercessory prayer
- Involve presenting the needs of others to God
- Act as a bridge for others
- Intervening or acting on behalf of another person
