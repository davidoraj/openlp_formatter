#Necessity of continued Devotion
==
#Devote yourself to read the Word
Ezra 7:10
  _For Ezra devoted himself to the study and observance of the law of the LORD, and to teaching its decrees and laws in_
  _Israel._
==
#Devote yourself to read the Word
1 Timothy 4:13,15
  _v.13 Till I come, give attention to reading, to exhortation, to doctrine_
  _v.15 Meditate on these things; give yourself entirely to them, that your progress may be evident to all._
==
#Devote yourself to prayer
Colossians 4:2
  _v.2 Continue earnestly in prayer, being vigilant in it with thanksgiving._
  _v.3 meanwhile praying also for us that God would open to us a door for the word, to speak the mystery of Christ, for_
  _which I am also in chains._
==
#Devote yourself to do good
Titus 3:14
  _Our people must learn to devote themselves to doing what is good, in order that they may provide for daily_
  _necessities and not live unproductive lives._
==
#Devote yourself to do good
Colossians 1:10
  _That you may walk worthy of the Lord, fully pleasing Him, being fruitful in every good work and increasing in the_
  _knowledge of God._
==
#Devote yourself to the work of the Lord
Nehemiah 5:16
  _Instead, I devoted myself to the work on this wall.  All my men were assembled there for the work; we did not_
  _acquire any land._
==
#Devote yourself to the work of the Lord
Nehemiah 6:2-3
  _v.2 Sanballat and Geshem sent me this message: “Come, let us meet together in one of the villages on the plain of Ono.”_
      _But they were scheming to harm me;_
  _v.3 So I sent messengers to them with this reply: “I am carrying on a great project and cannot go down. Why should_
      _the work stop while I leave it and go down to you?”_
==
#CONCLUSION:
Hebrew 3:7-9
  _v.7 Therefore, as the Holy Spirit says: “Today, if you will hear His voice,_
  _v.8 Do not harden your hearts as in the rebellion, in the day of trial in the wilderness,_
  _v.9 Where your fathers tested Me, tried Me, and saw My works forty years._
==

Psalm 95:7-9

  _v.8 “Do not harden your hearts, as in the rebellion, as in the day of trial in the wilderness_
  _v.9 When your fathers tested Me; they tried Me, though they saw My works._
