1. Anudinambunu prabhuni dalachuchu

Saantito jeevintamu

Verse 6:
6. Saktikoladi sareerabalamunu
Sramanu kreestuku nittumu (2)

Verse 7:
7. Chinnavaaralu daivaraajyapu
Chiruta vaarasulanchunu (2)

Verse 8:
8. Peddavaaralu daivajanulani
Permito bhaavintumu (2)
Suddudagu prabhu kreestu manasunu

Verse 9:
9. Jeevitambuna prabhunu premaa
