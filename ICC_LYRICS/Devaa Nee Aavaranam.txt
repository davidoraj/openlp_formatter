Devaa Nee Aavaranam
Chorus:
Devaa nee aavaranam
Maakento sreyaskaram (2)
Oka ghadiya ichata gaduputa melu
Veyi dinamula kantenu (2)

Verse 1:
1. Adbhuta kaaryamulu
Jariginchudevudavu (2)
Anavaratamu nee mahimanu pogada
Aatmalo nilupumayaa
Aatmato satyamuto
Aaraadhinchaga manasuto (2)
Aalpha omegayu aatmarupudavu
Anandinchaga nee odilo

Verse 2:
2. Atyanta parisuddamau
Needu gudaaramuna (2)
Nivasinchutaku maaku yogyata nosagi
Hechinchitivi nee dayan
Junthee dhaaralakannanu
Tene madhurima kannanu (2)
Sreshthamau needu vaakkula cheta
Mamu trupti parachumayaa

Verse 3:
3. Parisudda sannidhilo
Parisuddaatmuni needalo (2)
Paripurna hrudayamuto parivartanamuto
Prabhune prasthutinchedam
Maa dehame aalayam
Kaavaali neeke-nilayam (2)
Praana priyudavu padamula cheri
Paanaarpanamu chetunu
