Nee Raktamulo Nanu Kadigitivi
Chorus:
Nee raktamulo nanu kadigitivi
Parisuddhuni chesitivi
Nee raktamu naakai kaarchitivi
Nee praanamu nichitivi
Emittunayaa emittunayaa
Nee premaku saatiganu
Naa rakshana paatranu chebuni
Arpintu krutagnatalan

Verse 1:
1. Gata vatsaramu naa sodhanalo
Naa nindalu bhaaramulan
Tappinchitivi kaapaaditivi
Nanu premato nadipitivi

Verse 2:
2. Nee sannidhilo nanu cherchitivi
Nee premanu tala poyan
Nee gaayapu needalo daachitivi
Nee maargamu chupitivi

Verse 3:
3. Palu bhayamulu balamuga chuttukonan
Nee vabhayamu nichitivi
Ninu viduvanu mari yedabaayanani
Vaagdaanamulichitivi
