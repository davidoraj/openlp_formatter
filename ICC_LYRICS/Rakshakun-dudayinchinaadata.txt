Chorus:
Rakshakun-dudayinchinaa-data
Mana koraku parama
Rakshakun-dudayinchi-naadata
Rakshakun-dudayinchi-naadu
Raare golla boyalaara
Takshanamuna boyi mana ni
Reekshana phala mondedamu

Verse 1:
1. Daaveedu vansamandu
Dhanyudu janminchinaadu (2)
Devudagu yehovaa mana
Dikku deri chuchinaadu

Verse 2:
2. Gaganamu nundi diggi
Ghanudu gaabriyelu duta (2)
Taginattu cheppe vaariki
Migula santosha vaarta

Verse 3:
3. Vartamaanamu jeppi duta
Vaibhavamuna povuchunnaadu (2)
Kartanu juchina venuka
Kaantumu visramam bappudu

Verse 4:
4. Pasuvula tottilona
Bhaasillu vastramula jutti (2)
Sisuvunu ganugondurani
Seeghramuganu duta telpe

Verse 5:
5. Anuchu golla lokari kokaru
Aanavaalu jeppukonuchu (2)
Anumatinchi kadaku kreestu
Nandarikinee delpinaaru
