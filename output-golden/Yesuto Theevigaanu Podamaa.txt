Yesuto Theevigaanu Podamaa
Chorus:
Yesuto theevigaanu podamaa
Addugaa vachu vairi gelvanu
Yuddha-naa-dambuto bodamu

Chorus

Verse 1:
1. Raaraaju sainyamandu cheranu
Aa raaju divya seva cheyanu (2)
Yesu raaju mundugaa
Dhvajamu batti naduvagaa (2)
Yesuto theevigaanu vedalanu

Chorus

Verse 2:
2. Viswaasa kavacha-munu-dharinchuchu
Aa raaju naagna madini nilpuchu (2)
Anudinambu saktini
Pondu-chunna-vaaramai (2)
Yesuto theevigaanu vedalanu

Chorus

Verse 3:
3. Sodhanalu manala chutti vachinaa
Saataanu ambulenni tagilinaa (2)
Bhayamuledu manakika
Prabhuvu chenta nundumu (2)
Yesuto theevigaanu vedalanu

Chorus

Verse 4:
4. O yuvati yuvakulaaraa cherudi
Sree yesuraaju vaarta chaatudi (2)
Lokamanta ekamai
Yesunaathu golvanu (2)
Saadha-namb-evaru neevu nenegaa
