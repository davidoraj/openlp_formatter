Kummari O Kummari
Chorus:
Kummari o kummari
Jagatutpattidaari
Jigata mannaina naa vanka
Challaga chudumayyaa
Aa aa aa challaga chudumayyaa

Verse 1:
1. Panikiraani paatranani
Paaraveyakumaa
Pongi poralu paatragaa
Nannu nimpumaa

Verse 2:

Verse 3:
