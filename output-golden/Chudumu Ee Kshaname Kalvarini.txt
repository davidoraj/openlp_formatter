Chudumu Ee Kshaname Kalvarini
Chorus:
Chudumu ee kshaname kalvarini
Premaa-prabhuvu neekai niluchundenu
Goppa rakshananivva sree yesudu
Siluvalo vrelaadu chunnaadugaa (2)

Verse 1:
1. Maanavu lento chedipoyiri
Maraninche-damani talapoyaka (2)
Erugaru maranamu nikkamani
Narakam-unnadani vaarerugaru (2)

Chorus

Verse 2:
2. Ihamandu neeku kalavanniyu
Chanipovu samayaana ventaraavu (2)
Chanipoyi-nanu neevu lechedavu
Teerpunna-dani erugu oka dinamuna (2)

Chorus

Verse 3:
3. Manalanu dhanavan-tulugaa cheyanu
Daridru-daayenu mana prabhuvu (2)
Raktamu kaarchenu paapulakai
Angeeka-rinchumu sree yesuni (2)

Chorus

Verse 4:
4. Siluvapai chudumu aa priyuni
Aa premakai neevu yemittuvu
Arpinchuko needu jeevitamu
Aayana korakai jeevinchumu (2)
