O Neeti Suryudaa
Chorus:
O neeti suryudaa
Kreestesu naadhuda
Nee divya kaantini naalo
Udayimpa cheyu maa prabhu
Nannu veligimpumaa

Verse 1:
1. Nene lokaaniki
Velugai yunnaa-nani
Meeru lokaniki
Velugai yundaalani
Aade-sinchi-naavu-gaana
Naalo udayinchumaa prabhu
Nannu veliginchumaa

Chorus

Verse 2:
2. Naa antha-rangamuna
Ye mulan vedikina
Atyanta paapapu
Gaadaan-dhakaarame
Nannaa-varinche kaavuna
Naalo udayinchumaa prabhu
Nannu veliginchumaa

Chorus

Verse 4:
4. Nee jeeva vaakyamul
Chebuni ee tharam
Nee jeeva-jyotigaa
Prakaa-sinchu-natlu
Ee deena-paapin-maarcha-ganu
Naalo udayinchumaa prabhu
Nannu veliginchumaa

Chorus

Verse 5:
5. Naa jeevintambune
Tukambu vesina
Nee neeti traasulo
Saritu-ga-bonani
Ne nerigi-yinti-kaavuna
Naalo udayinchumaa prabhu
Nannu veliginchumaa
