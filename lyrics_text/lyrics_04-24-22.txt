1. Sing the wondrous love of Jesus

Chorus

Verse 3:

Just one glimpse of Him in glory
Will the toils of life repay

Chorus

Verse 4:

Soon His beauty we'll behold
Soon the pearly gates will open
We shall tread the streets of gold
